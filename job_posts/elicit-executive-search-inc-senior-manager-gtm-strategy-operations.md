# Elicit Executive Search, Inc. — Senior Manager, GTM Strategy & Operations

## Job Description

*Note: Posted by Elicit Executive Search, Inc. — actual hiring company is an unnamed AI startup.*

Senior Manager, GTM Strategy & Operations

We are a Tech AI-Startup, disrupting & transforming a $49B industry. We are in hyper-growth looking for a Senior Manager GTM Strategy & Operations to join the team. We have a global vision for our AI-enterprise product, and we are expanding to support our growth. (We have grown 3X!) And we're just getting started.

We are a fast-growing team that has helped scale companies like Facebook, Airbnb, Salesforce, Apple, and Square. We have been recognized by Forbes 50, as one of the Most Innovative & Fast-growing Company's.

As a member of the GTM Strategy & Operations team, you'll be a key partner to Sales, Marketing, and the broader executive team - driving strategic planning, operational execution, and analytical rigor across our revenue engine.

What You'll Do:
- Own GTM operations for a business unit: Lead day-to-day operations and long-term planning for one or more sales segments, including territory design, comp plans, sales metrics, reporting.
- Act as a strategic thought partner: Be the go-to partner for sales leadership and execs — solving complex problems, framing key decisions, and bringing structure to ambiguity.
- Drive GTM cadence and accountability: Run the operating rhythm — owning pipeline reviews, forecast calls, QBRs, and leadership syncs.
- Lead cross-functional execution: Work across Sales, Marketing, CS, Product, and Finance to drive strategic initiatives forward.
- Build with data: Use SQL, spreadsheets, and BI tools to structure analysis and uncover insights that improve GTM execution.
- Own special projects: From compensation design to rep productivity modeling to account segmentation.

Qualifications:
- 8+ years of experience in sales strategy, revenue operations, consulting, investing, or a similar high-performance environment
- Strong analytical and modeling skills — fluent in Excel/Sheets and can write SQL or willing to learn
- Deep understanding of go-to-market motions and SaaS business models
- Experience working closely with GTM systems like Salesforce, Outreach, Clari, and Gong
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- High bias for action; technically inclined; collaborative mindset
