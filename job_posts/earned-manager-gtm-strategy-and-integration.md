# Earned — Manager, GTM Strategy and Integration

## Job Description

Job Summary
Earned is seeking a Manager, GTM Strategy and Integration to drive revenue acceleration across newly acquired firms. This role will play a critical execution and leadership role in integrating newly acquired firms and activating growth across the Earned platform.

This role reports to the Chief Growth Officer and partners closely with Corporate Development, Client Development, Marketing, Operations, Product/Technology, Finance, HR, and leaders at newly acquired firms. The primary focus is ensuring that acquired businesses are operationally ready, commercially enabled, and successfully activated, while building strong, collaborative relationships with sellers, firm leaders, and client-facing teams navigating change to cross-serve Earned's broader service suite—most often wealth management—into their existing client base.

GTM Strategy and Integration is a core value-creation engine at Earned. This role sits at the intersection of strategy and execution, translating deal theses and growth plans into real, measurable outcomes. The Manager will own day-to-day activation execution for assigned firms while contributing to the ongoing refinement of Earned's activation playbooks, operating cadence, and analytics.

This is a hands-on, high-judgment role that requires strong executive presence, analytical rigor, and the ability to operate effectively in fast-moving, ambiguous environments. It requires comfort operating across complex organizational dynamics, multiple stakeholders, and differing firm cultures during periods of rapid transition.

Key Responsibilities

Growth Activation & Integration Execution
- Partner with Corporate Development and Growth leadership to translate investment theses into clear, measurable activation goals for newly acquired firms.
- Build strong working relationships with sellers, firm leaders, and Relationship Managers to create shared ownership of growth outcomes.
- Partner with inherited teams to translate Earned's growth strategy into practical, firm-specific execution that preserves trust with existing clients.
- Serve as the primary activation lead for assigned acquisitions, coordinating cross-functional workstreams required to achieve successful ramp and stabilization.
- Assess current-state capabilities of acquired firms across people, process, data, and technology to inform activation approach.
- Lead onboarding, coaching, and enablement of Relationship Managers and client-facing teams to drive confidence and adoption of Earned offerings.
- Ensure acquired firms are operationally ready to execute cross-serve motions within defined timelines.

Cross-Serve Enablement & Playbook Execution
- Deploy, test, and refine Earned's cross-serve playbooks to attach wealth management and other services to existing client relationships.
- Partner with Client Development, Marketing, and Relationship Managers to orchestrate positioning, enablement, and execution of cross-serve efforts.
- Ensure sales processes, workflows, and enablement tools are embedded in CRM and supporting systems.
- Proactively identify friction points in execution and surface recommendations to improve effectiveness, speed, and consistency.

Analytics, Reporting & Continuous Improvement
- Design and operationalize analytics and reporting to track activation progress, cross-serve performance, and value creation.
- Monitor performance against activation goals and identify opportunities for course correction or acceleration.
- Use data to inform sequencing, prioritization, and refinement of activation tactics.
- Contribute to the evolution of Earned's activation operating model and tooling based on real-world execution learnings.

Cross-Functional Coordination
- Partner closely with Operations, Finance, HR, Technology, and CRM teams and leaders at acquired firms to navigate organizational complexity and align on priorities during ramp and activation.
- Support people-related integration efforts including training, role clarity, and operating cadence where needed.
- Communicate progress, risks, and outcomes clearly to Growth leadership and stakeholders.

Key Requirements
- Bachelor's degree required; MBA preferred
- 5–10 years of experience in growth strategy, revenue operations, go-to-market execution, or management consulting
- Proven ability to lead complex, cross-functional initiatives and deliver measurable results
- Demonstrated ability to build trust and influence outcomes with senior stakeholders and inherited teams in periods of change
- Strong analytical capability with comfort designing metrics, dashboards, and performance tracking
- High judgment operator who can work independently and manage ambiguity
- Experience with Salesforce and related sales/marketing enablement tools (Outreach, Gong, Looker, Excel/Google Sheets strongly preferred)
- Project management experience, including use of tools such as Asana
- Top-tier management consulting, banking, or PE-backed operating experience preferred
- Experience in financial services, healthcare, or multi-service organizations a plus
- Willingness to travel 2–3x per month, with increased travel during post-acquisition ramp periods

About Earned
Earned is a category-defining, first-in-kind tax-smart financial services firm dedicated to serving doctors, their families, and their practices. Our goal is to be the only financial partner doctors need by seamlessly integrating personal and practice-based solutions to maximize their wealth potential and drive better financial outcomes.

We bring together tax, accounting, wealth management, insurance, and legal services under one platform not as a generic one-stop shop, but to deliver better advice through an integrated view of a doctor's full financial life. Our differentiation is relationship-led, trust-based selling paired with disciplined execution across the ecosystem.

Earned manages $3.4B+ in assets, serves more than 20,000 clients, and is one of the fastest-growing doctor-focused platforms in the country. Backed by $200M of committed capital, we are scaling rapidly through acquisitions and organic growth across multiple service lines.
