Position Overview:The Business Development Manager of Strategic Partnerships will play a pivotal role in expanding the company’s reach into non-traditional business markets, including pharmaceutical, medical device, and B2B segments. The role requires a dynamic, analytical, and highly collaborative leader who will cultivate strategic partnerships to drive growth, while also assisting the broader organization in adopting existing solutions such as the Accelerate program to unlock additional service opportunities.

Strategic PartnershipsDevelop, negotiate, and manage strategic partnerships that drive incremental growth and innovation.Build long-term relationships with key external stakeholders and partners.Serve as a connector between internal teams and external partners to create mutually beneficial collaborations.
Internal Enablement & GrowthPartner with internal teams to facilitate the adoption of solutions such as Accelerate to expand services growth.Provide cross-functional leadership to drive alignment and integration of new offerings across the organization.Act as a champion for innovation and change, helping the organization adapt and grow.
