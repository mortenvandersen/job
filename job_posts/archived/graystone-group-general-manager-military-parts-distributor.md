# Graystone Group — General Manager (Military Parts Distributor)

## Job Description

General Manager, Military Parts DistributionLocation: Remote (U.S.) | Some Travel RequiredCompensation: $200,000–$500,000 Total Cash Comp + Equity
Company OverviewGraystone Group is partnered with a venture-backed defense logistics platform to identify a senior operator to lead and scale its distribution business.
Our client is executing a high-growth strategy combining AI-powered procurement technology with the acquisition and integration of aerospace and defense parts distributors. With significant institutional capital and an active M&A pipeline, the platform is positioned to rapidly scale from a single operating entity into a fully integrated, multi-entity distribution enterprise.
Position OverviewGraystone Group is seeking a General Manager, Defense Distribution Division to own and scale a rapidly growing defense distribution platform.
This individual will serve as the P&L owner and operational leader, responsible for driving revenue growth, margin expansion, and operational excellence across procurement, sales, fulfillment, and post-acquisition integration.
This is a true build-and-scale leadership role, with the mandate to grow the business from approximately $10M to $500M+ in revenue through a combination of organic growth and strategic acquisitions.
Key ResponsibilitiesOwn full P&L responsibility including revenue, gross margin, EBITDA, and cash conversion Build, recruit, and lead a high-performing outside sales organization Oversee procurement operations, including supplier management, inventory strategy, and fulfillment execution Develop and expand relationships with OEMs and authorized distributors Lead post-acquisition integration of acquired businesses into a unified operating platform Drive pricing strategy and margin optimization, leveraging advanced data and AI-enabled tools Build and scale operational infrastructure, including warehouse, logistics, compliance, and quality systems (AS9120, ISO 9001) Manage executive-level relationships with DoD agencies, prime contractors, and key suppliers Develop repeatable integration playbooks to support continued M&A activity Report on core KPIs including revenue growth, margin performance, pipeline health, and integration milestones 
Qualifications10+ years of experience in defense or aerospace distribution, industrial distribution, or defense supply chain operations 3+ years of P&L ownership in a leadership role (GM, VP Operations, President, or equivalent) Proven experience building and managing outside sales teams (hiring, compensation design, territory planning, quota setting) Deep understanding of DoD procurement processes, contract vehicles, pricing dynamics, and compliance requirements Experience integrating acquired businesses or leading large-scale operational transformation initiatives Familiarity with quality systems (AS9120, ISO 9001) and defense logistics/packaging standards Strong ability to operate alongside technology and engineering teams, leveraging data and automation to drive outcomes Demonstrated track record of scaling a distribution or services business through both organic growth and M&A 
Profile of SuccessThis role is best suited for a senior operator currently leading or having led a defense or industrial distribution business, or a private equity-backed platform leader with experience integrating and scaling portfolio companies.The ideal candidate brings a combination of commercial leadership, operational rigor, and M&A integration experience, with a clear understanding that the defense distribution market is undergoing rapid consolidation and technological transformation.
Compensation & BenefitsTotal Compensation: $200,000–$500,000 (Base + Performance Bonus) Meaningful equity participation aligned with long-term enterprise value creation Comprehensive benefits package
