# Chick-fil-A Restaurants — General Manager / Executive Multi Unit Restaurant Director

## Job Description

Multi-Unit Executive General Manager / Director – Southwest Texas
Chick-fil-A, a leading quick-service brand known for exceptional service and operational excellence, is seeking an experienced Multi-Unit Executive General Manager to oversee operations for two high-volume restaurants in Southwest Texas.
We rarely seek external candidates for leadership roles at this level, making this a unique opportunity to join a premier Owner/Operator and contribute at the multi-unit stage.
Key Requirements:Prior management experience within Chick-fil-A is required (resumes without this will not be considered)Proven track record in high-volume restaurant operations, team leadership, and driving resultsWillingness to relocate to the Southwest Texas regionBilingual English / Spanish Preferred
Responsibilities:Full P&L accountability for two locationsLead restaurant teams to deliver outstanding guest experiences and operational excellencePartner closely with the Owner/Operator to achieve growth goals and uphold Chick-fil-A standardsDrive sales, profitability, and team development
Compensation:Salary approximately $150,000 (negotiable based on experience) with an opportunity to earn in excess of $200,000 with the right experience and ability to deliver results. Significant incentive compensation tied to business growth and performance metricsComprehensive benefits package
This role offers long-term growth potential with one of the most respected brands in the industry. Only highly qualified candidates should apply.Applications will be handled with strict confidentiality by our executive search firm. Please submit your resume and a brief note on your Chick-fil-A experience
