Job Title: Head of North AmericaPosition Type: FTELocation: RemoteSalary Range: USD $ 170，000- 220，000Job ID#: 164722
A founder-led, consumer electronics-native brand that has engineered a bold category expansion — bringing tech-first thinking into the premium home living space. This is a tech company redefining how North American consumers experience their homes.Backed by serious capital and a track record in consumer electronics innovation, they are now scaling North American offline and omnichannel operations.
