# BAGGU — Senior Digital Operations Manager

## Job Description

About UsBAGGU is a design focused bag company based in San Francisco, California. We are a small but hard-working team of people dedicated to making cool bags that feel good to use.For more information on our company visit us at www.baggu.com or follow us on Instagram @baggu.Please note that info@baggu.com is used for Customer Experience inquiries - please do not use this channel for Application inquiries.
About the RoleBAGGU is seeking a Senior Digital Operations Manager to lead our Digital Operations team and strengthen the digital backbone of our growing brand.
In this role, you will drive the evolution of our internal infrastructure, translating business goals into scalable solutions, and ensure tools are successfully adopted into day to day workflows. Working closely alongside cross team Senior Managers and VP of Operations, this is a collaborative role that calls for both strategic thinking, hands-on problem solving and people leadership.
As a manager, you will cultivate a diverse team, coaching members with varying levels of experience. You will set the standard for execution, guiding the team through the nuances of project delivery, testing protocols, and stakeholder communication.
LocationRemote but limited to candidates in the following states: CA, OR, NM, PA, IL, NJ, MI, NY, VA
ResponsibilitiesDrive the internal digital roadmap: Lead the evaluation, selection, and implementation of internal software systems, ensuring our tech stack evolves to meet the needs of a scaling organization. Striking the right balance between necessary complexity and lean efficiency.Spearhead cross-functional discovery: Partner with department stakeholders to diagnose workflow bottlenecks, translating complex operational challenges into streamlined digital solutions.Orchestrate system implementation: Oversee the full lifecycle of new system rollouts—from requirements gathering and solution design to user acceptance testing (UAT) and successful adoption.Cultivate and lead the team: Manage and mentor a mix of Project Managers and Digital Operations Leads. You will set the standard for execution, guiding your team through the nuances of project delivery, testing protocols, and stakeholder communication.Champion change management: rigorous documentation, training, and support structures to ensure high adoption rates and "digital fluency" across the company.Ensure operational integrity: Establish KPIs for system performance and project success, ensuring that all digital initiatives deliver measurable improvements in efficiency and data accuracy.
Requirements3+ years of management experience marked by a proven ability to build and motivate high-performing cross-functional teams.Digitally native, with intuitive fluency in modern software ecosystems (i.e., ERPs, PLMs, Project Management tools, and No-Code automations).Proven success in system management: experience leading design and rolling out new digital workflows across teams. (NetSuite workflow, and Celigo connector experience preferred)Exceptional critical thinking and problem-solving skills: You can deconstruct complex workflows into logical steps and identify the root cause of inefficiencies.Strong stakeholder management: You are an articulate communicator who can translate technical concepts for non-technical teams and build consensus across the organization.Project Management expertise: Deep understanding of cross-team coordination, project management methodologies, resource planning, and timeline management - able to deliver on results efficiently
Nice to HavesTechnical NetSuite and Celigo experience or custom solutions (SuiteScripts, etc.)Ecommerce experienceExperience working with IT/cyber-security services or building solutionsA portfolio of internal systems or workflows you have successfully designed, rolled out, and optimized.
