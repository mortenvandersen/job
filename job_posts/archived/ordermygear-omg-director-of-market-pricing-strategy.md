# OrderMyGear (OMG) — Director of Market & Pricing Strategy

## Job Description

Inktavo + OrderMyGearInktavo and OrderMyGear (OMG) have joined forces to modernize how the apparel and promotional product decoration industry does business. Since the early 2000s, our combined platforms have provided a better way for thousands of distributors, decorators, and team dealers to sell branded merchandise across channels. Today, we are the market-leading technology shaping the future of group online commerce.
By joining our team, you’ll play a pivotal role in this rapidly changing industry as we scale our platforms and improve the eCommerce experience for our clients and their customers.
Market & Pricing Strategy @ MergeCo
As we evolve into the next chapter of our company, we are sharpening where we play, whom we serve, and how we win. The Director of Market & Pricing Strategy will play a critical role in that effort by grounding decisions in market reality, ensuring our product roadmap decisions are aimed at the right customers, in the right segments, with a differentiated value proposition, and ensuring we have a consistent framework across the organization.
This role sits within the Product organization and reports to the Chief Product Officer.
The ideal candidate is a market and pricing strategist who operates like a thought and product leader, someone who brings clarity to ambiguous questions and influences decisions without owning the roadmap.
You are deeply curious about customers, markets, and value creation, and are motivated by helping teams decide where to play and why, not by defining features or managing delivery.You have experience in roles such as market strategy, pricing strategy, corporate strategy, or management consulting, with hands-on exposure to product-led businesses. Experience in high-growth B2B SaaS, vertical SaaS, marketplaces, or post-acquisition environments is strongly preferred.
