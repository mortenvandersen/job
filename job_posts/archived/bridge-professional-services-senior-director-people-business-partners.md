# Bridge Professional Services — Senior Director. People Business Partners

## Job Description

A global leader in insurance technology, is seeking a Senior Director, People Business Partners – North America to help shape the future of work at the company and strengthen the experience of ~2,200 team members across the region. This strategic leader will oversee a team of five Senior People Business Partners. This role is key in ensuring our people strategies enable our business strategy. As a member of the People Leadership Team, you will empower leaders, elevate organizational capability, and build an environment where talent thrives and teams can do the best work of their careers. You will guide the North America PBP operating model and ensure our people practices fuel performance, scalability, and long‑term company success. You’ll partner with COEs and collaborate with regional People leaders globally to deliver solutions that enhance growth, belonging, leadership effectiveness, and cultural evolution. This role plays a central part in shaping how our organization grows: removing barriers, building high‑trust relationships, and enabling organization’s transformation through its people.
