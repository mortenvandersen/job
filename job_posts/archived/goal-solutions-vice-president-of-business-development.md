# Goal Solutions — Vice President of Business Development

## Job Description

Bring Your Curiosity. Bring Your Drive. Bring Your Leadership.
At Goal Solutions, we are transforming the consumer finance industry through technology, data, and innovative servicing solutions.As our company continues to grow, we are looking for a Vice President of Business Development to lead strategic revenue growth initiatives and expand our partnerships across fintech originators, investment firms, banks, and credit unions.
This is a highly visible executive role responsible for identifying, developing, and closing high-value partnerships that fuel long-term company growth.
How This Role Fits InOur leadership team is focused on building a high-performing, forward-thinking organization that delivers exceptional servicing solutions to clients across consumer lending markets.
The VP of Business Development plays a critical role in expanding Goal’s market presence by developing new client relationships, strengthening strategic partnerships, and driving measurable revenue growth.
You will collaborate closely with executive leadership, operations, marketing, and product teams to ensure new opportunities align with Goal’s long-term strategy and operational capabilities.
Impact You'll Make in This Role· Drive measurable revenue growth through strategic client acquisition and expansion of key partnerships· Build and maintain a robust pipeline of enterprise-level opportunities across fintech, banking, and capital markets· Strengthen Goal’s brand positioning within the consumer loan servicing marketplace· Establish repeatable, scalable sales processes aligned with executive growth strategy· Cultivate long-term strategic client relationships that generate sustainable recurring revenue· Serve as a trusted advisor to clients, helping them design servicing solutions that support their growth
What You'll Do
Strategic Business Development & Revenue Growth· Develop and execute territory and market strategies aligned with company growth goals· Identify and prioritize high-value prospects across fintech, banks, credit unions, and investment firms· Lead complex enterprise sales cycles from discovery through contract execution and implementation· Maintain disciplined outreach cadence to generate consistent pipeline activity· Represent Goal Solutions at industry conferences and networking opportunities
Consulting & Relationship Management· Conduct executive-level discoveries to understand client objectives and servicing gaps· Present customized financial servicing solutions and platform capabilities· Build strong executive relationships throughout extended sales cycles· Maintain ongoing communication with clients to ensure long-term partnership success
Sales Operations & Pipeline Management· Utilize Salesforce and CRM tools to track pipeline health, forecasting, and performance metrics· Provide market intelligence and sales insights to executive leadership· Collaborate with marketing to refine messaging and prospect engagement strategies
Cross-Functional Leadership· Partner with operations, finance, compliance, and executive leadership to evaluate new opportunities· Ensure alignment between client solutions and operational capabilities
What You Bring· Proven success leading enterprise B2B sales within fintech, lending, or financial services· Experience selling loan servicing, lending platforms, or financial infrastructure solutions· Demonstrated ability to generate pipeline and close complex, long-cycle deals· Strong executive presence and consultative selling approach· Exceptional relationship-building and negotiation skills· Ability to travel approximately 35%
Minimum Qualifications· Experience in consumer servicing
Preferred Qualifications· Established network within consumer lending or fintech ecosystems· Experience working with investment firms, banks, or credit unions· Experience negotiating LOIs, servicing agreements, or partnership contracts· Experience driving revenue growth within a scaling fintech organization
Essential Functions· Develop and execute strategic sales plans aligned with organizational growth targets· Lead complex B2B enterprise sales cycles from prospecting through implementation· Analyze market trends, financial data, and competitive landscapes to inform strategy· Communicate effectively with executive-level clients and internal stakeholders· Negotiate high-value contracts and partnership agreements· Manage and prioritize multiple long-cycle opportunities simultaneously
Why You'll Love Working HerePeople stay at Goal Solutions because they can grow, innovate, lead, and make meaningful impact.We invest in our people so they can deliver exceptional results for our clients.
Benefits include · Budgeted annual salary: $180,000· Competitive salary + bonus eligibility· 401(k) with 4% company match· Medical, dental, vision, life insurance, disability, and critical illness coverage· Annual HSA contribution: $1,700· 14 paid holidays every year, including your birthday!· Free snacks and beverages in the office· Paid Parental Leave· Tuition reimbursement· $2,000 Vacation Incentive + $1000 Sabbatical after 3 years
About Goal Solutions Goal Solutions (“Goal” or the “Company”) is an innovative financial services company delivering comprehensive and customizable Primary and Backup Servicing, Asset Management, and SPV Administration solutions. Driven by technology, data science, and industry expertise, Goal is a strategic partner across the entire consumer finance lifecycle. Our experienced leadership team has worked collaboratively for over a decade, managing over $42B in consumer assets.
Directly or through its subsidiaries, GSS Data Services, Launch Servicing (“Launch”) and Turnstile Capital Management (“TCM”), Goal serves key markets such as residential solar, home improvement, student finance, and a growing number of personal loan providers. Currently, Goal supports a variety of clients including hedge funds, traditional banks, ABS structures, insurance companies, investment banks, and colleges and universities.
Goal will continue to grow the business both organically and through acquisitions. Senior Goal leadership has been working together for over fifteen years and we pride ourselves and our work on our mission, values, culture, and service to our community. Our company is headquartered in San Diego, CA and has been named “Best Places to Work” by the San Diego Business Journal since 2015.
