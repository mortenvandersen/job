# Typhur Robotics — General Manager

## Job Description

Company: Typhur Robotics — a home robotics company focused on the kitchen. We build intelligent kitchen systems for everyday meals and drinks—starting with precision cooking products you can buy today, and advancing step by step toward kitchen autonomy.

Role Summary: Typhur Robotics is hiring a U.S. President & General Manager to own the U.S. business end-to-end. You will be the U.S. P&L owner and the operating leader across GTM strategy, marketing, sales, customer experience, fulfillment/3PL operations, and channel partnerships.

What You'll Own:
- U.S. P&L Ownership: Full responsibility for U.S. revenue, margin, and operating budget. Build forecasting, operating cadence, and KPI dashboards.
- GTM Strategy & Growth: Define U.S. go-to-market strategy across Amazon, DTC, and retail/channel. Own launch plans, pricing, promotions, and growth experiments.
- Marketing (U.S.): Performance marketing and funnel optimization. Retail media / Amazon ads strategy. Brand positioning execution.
- Sales & Channel Partnerships: Own revenue across key channels: Amazon, DTC, retail/distributors, strategic partners. Build and lead lean but scalable U.S. sales organization.
- Customer Experience: Own end-to-end customer journey. Build CX metrics and workflows.
- Operations / Fulfillment: Lead U.S. fulfillment strategy: 3PL selection, SLAs, cost control. Own reverse logistics and returns operations.
- Team & Leadership: Hire, lead, and develop a high-performing U.S. team (marketing, sales, CX, ops).

Must-have Qualifications:
- 12+ years in consumer, e-commerce, marketplace, or adjacent GTM leadership roles
- Proven P&L ownership and ability to drive topline growth + operational excellence
- Deep experience across at least two of: Amazon marketplace, DTC, retail/channel partnerships
- Strong cross-functional leadership: marketing + sales + CX + ops
- Fluent English; fluent Mandarin Chinese (business/professional level) — must be able to operate in both languages with U.S. teams and China HQ

Nice-to-have:
- Experience in consumer hardware / appliances / smart home / robotics-adjacent categories
- Experience at high-scale organizations (Amazon/Google/Meta) and in startup/scale-up environments
