# VIVO Agency — Director of Strategy - Healthcare/Pharma Marketing

## Job Description

About UsWe’re an independent creative agency that helps healthcare’s biggest game changers connect with HCPs, patients, and consumers. From breakthrough campaigns to high-impact sales enablement, our smart, strategic teams do more than “get it done.” We uncover the path to success — and inspire our clients to walk it with us.
About YouVIVO is seeking a Director of Strategy who brings curiosity, clarity, and conviction to every challenge. If you’re ready to shape markets, inspire great work, and guide teams with a mix of rigor and heart — this is your place!You’re a natural problem-solver, translating healthcare complexity into actionable direction. You’re energized by learning new categories, asking sharp questions, and helping clients get to the truth of what their audiences need. You’re as comfortable diagnosing a business challenge as you are inspiring a room during a pitch. You know how to build trust — with clients, with creatives, and with cross-functional partners. You balance confidence with humility and gratitude, and you believe the best ideas come from teams that collaborate openly and treat each other with respect. You care deeply about craft, but even more about impact. You thrive in an agency that values initiative over ego, progress over perfection, and inclusion, integrity, and curiosity above all.
What You’ll DoAdvise clients for brands reaching consumer and HCP audiences, helping them navigate complex categoriesTranslate business challenges into clear hypotheses, strategic options, and a winning path forwardPartner closely with client services and creative to develop work that shifts perceptions and market shareIdentify and cultivate opportunities to help clients achieve their goalsBecome an expert in designated medical specialities/categories and grow the firm’s collective knowledge Bring data fluency to analyzing opportunities, developing goals, and measuring successAdvance the strategy discipline by contributing to our toolset, processes, and ways of thinkingBe a culture carrier: live the VIVO Core Values, promote inclusion and equity, celebrate great work, and support your teammates generously
What You Bring7+ years of experience in marketing/creative strategy, with 3+ years in healthcare verticals2+ years strategically leading work at a marketing/creative agency (client presentation, writing creative briefs)Experience launching medical products and/or working with healthcare providersDemonstrated ability to advise clients — identifying options and helping them commit to a directionExperience analyzing categories — market sizing/growth, company and competitive assessmentExperience leading market research — like IDIs, focus groups, surveys, and synthesizingExceptional communication skills — written, verbal, and visual — with the ability to simplify complexityProficiency with tools like G Suite, Slack, Zoom, and common research platformsWillingness to travel occasionally for client and agency meetings (up to ~25%)
Inclusion @ VIVOAt VIVO, we believe bold ideas make game-changing health innovation accessible to all. Therefore, inclusion and equality aren’t just values — they’re our advantage. We believe stronger ideas come from a mix of perspectives, and we’re dedicated to creating a space where everyone feels empowered to bring their authentic selves to work.
