# Octave — VP of Strategic Partnerships

## Job Description

Octave is a modern behavioral health practice creating a new standard for care delivery that's both high-quality and accessible. With in-person and virtual clinics in multiple states, the company offers evidence-based individual, couples, and family therapy, while pioneering relationships with payers to make care more affordable through insurance. By raising the bar on how care is delivered and how providers are supported, we are building a sustainable system that values equity, affordability, and effectiveness.

**Job Summary**

The VP of Strategic Partnerships is an entrepreneurial and experienced leader that will help the company get closer to its mission of expanding access to quality mental health services by building and overseeing an existing business line. This person is responsible for the end-to-end strategy and execution of this venture, including external relationship management, partnering with internal stakeholders to ensure successful planning and operations along with reporting on business metrics to ensure alignment with overall company goals. This is a critical role for the company and one that has high visibility. This role requires someone who is mission-driven, hands-on, and comfortable with a fast pace and ambiguity.

**Management Responsibilities**
- Creating change in the department that is beneficial to the company as a whole
- Allocating budgets and implementing policies and procedures to achieve set goals
- Develop and execute long-term organizational strategies to achieve company objectives
- Provide guidance, support, and mentorship to promote high performance and professional development
- Making key decisions regarding resource allocation, budgeting, policies, and operational procedures
- Develop key performance indicators (KPIs), and strategies to achieve operational excellence
- Effectively communicate the organization's goals, strategies, and performance to stakeholders

**Duties/Responsibilities**
- Report on and be accountable for outcomes and business metrics related to the new venture
- Support and orchestrate cross-functional teams to design and execute an existing venture in partnership with an external entity / partner
- Partner & drive communications with the external entity to understand requirements and drive consensus internally and externally
- Collaborate with other internal teams in a complex, multi-layered environment to drive execution
- Refine and iterate on process and design to further strengthen the partner relationship and drive continued growth

**Required Skills**
- Strong financial acumen: comfort with and inclination to lead with data and facts
- Strong presentation skills, including demonstrated success presenting and communicating with other executives
- Strong written and oral communication skills
- Experience working in a high growth environment
- Experience and comfort with working with ambiguity
- Strong analytical skills, can translate complex data into clear action

**Education & Experience**
- 10+ years of experience. Prior success in expanding businesses (in tech, operations, strategy, consulting, banking, or related experience)
- 5+ years of people management experience
- Experience starting or scaling a successful startup, ideally in the healthcare space
- Experience working directly with product development and engineering teams

**Compensation**
- Zone 2/3 (CA): $238,800 – $270,000 base + equity (stock options) + annual bonus
