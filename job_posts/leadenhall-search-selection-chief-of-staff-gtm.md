# Leadenhall Search & Selection (AI Startup) — Chief of Staff - GTM

## Job Description

Leadenhall Search and Selection are recruiting on behalf of one of the fastest growing AI Startups. They are seeking a Chief of Staff - GTM that can generate new business and turn them into qualified pipeline opportunities.

**Responsibilities**
- Drive top-of-funnel growth by securing high-value meetings with large enterprise organizations and progressing them into sales-qualified opportunities
- Cultivate and manage partnerships with venture investors to unlock warm access to emerging companies within their portfolios
- Design and operationalize scalable outbound systems, including campaign structure, messaging frameworks, and audience segmentation
- Collaborate directly with executive leadership to inform and refine pricing approaches for complex, high-value deals based on frontline insights

**Requirements**
- Experience at a top tier investment bank or a management consulting firm
- Ability to build relationships with senior executives
- Strong communication & interpersonal skills - experience of representing the CEO in a variety of settings

**What's on offer**
- Equity ownership
- High speed culture
- Very strong compensation packages with high bonuses
- Collaborative, growth-focused culture
- Employee development
- Healthcare/dental/vision
- Paid vacation/sick time, paid holidays
- 401k and profit sharing
