# Swooped — Chief of Staff (AI Shopping Agent)

## Job Description

Note: Swooped is a Talent Platform (not a staffing agency). The actual employer is an unnamed AI Shopping Agent company.

About the Opportunity
The company is the top-performing AI Shopping Agent, delivering the best products from across the web with unmatched accuracy, quality, and trust.

The Role
The organization is looking for a Chief of Staff to the CEO who can manage the CEO's priorities with obsessive follow-through and who has the executive presence and judgment to operate as her proxy when needed. This is not a traditional Executive Assistant role, nor is it a purely strategic one.

This individual will serve as the CEO's right hand, her early warning system, and her force multiplier. If the CEO is in a room, this person will either be in it or will have already prepared her for it. If an action item leaves a meeting, this person will own making sure it gets done. This role is ideal for someone who is equally comfortable building a board deck, drafting a high-stakes email on the CEO's behalf, and relentlessly chasing down a direct report for an overdue update. The role reports directly to the CEO and operates with a high degree of visibility and trust across the organization.

What You'll Do
- Serve as a trusted extension of the CEO — drafting, responding to, and managing email correspondence on her behalf with sound judgment and her voice.
- Manage and protect the CEO's calendar with a strong point of view on what deserves her time, what doesn't, and what can be delegated.
- Prepare the CEO for every meeting, conversation, and commitment with briefings, context, talking points, and pre-reads.
- Own follow-up with the CEO's direct reports and executive team on goals, priorities, and commitments, relentlessly and diplomatically.
- Drive weekly syncs, 1:1 rhythms, and cross-functional check-ins: own the agenda, take the notes, and make sure every action item has an owner and a deadline.
- Track progress against company and CEO-level goals; surface blockers and delays before they become problems.
- Build and refine presentations for internal and external audiences (exec team, board, all-hands, etc.).
- Identify opportunities to improve how the CEO's office operates and implement lightweight systems to support them.

Ideal Background
- 4–7 years of experience in a Chief of Staff, executive operations, consulting, or similar high-accountability role — startup or high-growth company experience strongly preferred.
- Comfortable holding senior people accountable; experience managing up, across, and down without relying on formal authority.
- Exceptional writer, able to draft clearly and quickly, and adapt their voice to represent someone else at the executive level.
- Experience building polished presentations for executive and board-level audiences.
- High judgment and discretion when handling sensitive information about people, strategy, and the business.

Compensation & Benefits
- Expected base salary: $155,000–$200,000 USD
- Equity in the form of stock options
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- 401(k) plan
- Flexible PTO and company holidays
- Fully remote within the United States
- Periodic company offsites and team gatherings
