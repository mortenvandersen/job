# Merraine Group — General Manager / Single-Threaded Owner (STO)

## Job Description

A mission-driven organization operating at the intersection of technology, media, research, and philanthropy is seeking a General Manager / Single-Threaded Owner (STO) to design and execute a major initiative focused on narrative dynamics within the American information ecosystem. This role is built for a high-agency operator who can translate strategy into action. The GM will own a portfolio of initiatives aimed at identifying and influencing high-impact narratives, mobilizing credible messengers, and strengthening the ecosystem's ability to respond to antisemitism, anti-Western ideology, misinformation, manipulation, and coordinated influence campaigns. You will operate as the accountable owner, setting direction, building systems, and delivering measurable outcomes.

About the Organization: The organization is a new initiative backed by leading technologists, business leaders, and philanthropists working to counter the growing influence of antisemitism, anti-Western ideology, and coordinated misinformation. While many organizations are working in this space, most were built for a slower, less adversarial information environment. This initiative exists to modernize how these challenges are addressed — equipping the ecosystem with the data, tools, partnerships, and strategic capabilities required to respond to rapidly evolving narratives, including those that undermine democratic values and target Jewish communities. The organization combines the mission focus of a nonprofit with the agility of a startup and is led by operators from technology, media, finance, and growth-stage companies.

What You'll Own:
- Identify high-leverage narrative opportunities, particularly those shaping perceptions around antisemitism and Western institutions
- Develop messaging that resonates with key audiences and counters harmful ideological narratives
- Build and activate networks of creators, influencers, and trusted messengers
- Detect and expose coordinated, foreign-driven, or ideologically motivated influence operations
- Build real-time narrative intelligence and research capabilities
- Partner with media outlets, platforms, and institutions to shape accurate, values-aligned narratives
- Deploy rapid-response efforts during major events or narrative inflection points

Key Responsibilities:
- Serve as Single-Threaded Owner of a strategic initiative from concept through execution
- Develop narrative strategies and campaign frameworks addressing antisemitism, anti-Western sentiment, and misinformation
- Recruit and activate creators, researchers, and ecosystem partners
- Build systems for data, monitoring, and real-time insights
- Coordinate rapid-response messaging during major narrative shifts or crises
- Establish clear metrics, accountability, and operational discipline

Ideal Background: Strong candidates may come from technology, media, startups, communications, policy, or public-interest organizations and will likely bring experience in areas such as: strategy and operations leadership, narrative strategy or communications campaigns, digital media ecosystems and creator networks, counter-misinformation/extremism/influence analysis, data-driven decision making.

Core Traits: Owner mentality; speed and execution; strategic judgment; operational rigor; network builder.

Compensation: $200,000–$250,000/yr. Posted via Merraine Group (recruiting firm).
