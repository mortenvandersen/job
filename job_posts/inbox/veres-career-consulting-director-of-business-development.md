# Veres Career Consulting — Director of Business Development

## Job Description

Our client is a specialist technology consultancy with over 15 years of experience and a team of more than 200 experts across Europe and North America. Trusted by major international insurers, the company takes AI and data capabilities and turns them into real, production-grade products: tools that make underwriting faster, claims smarter, and customer journeys genuinely better. With a well-established international footprint and a strong track record in the insurance sector, our client is now investing heavily to grow its US presence. To lead that growth, our client is hiring a Director of Business Development: a senior commercial professional with an active network, deep knowledge of the insurance market, and a hunger to open new doors and close meaningful deals in the US.
What You'll Be Doing
• Identifying and pursuing new business opportunities across US carriers, brokers, and InsurTechs, reading market signals and turning them into a healthy, growing pipeline.• Taking ownership of the full commercial conversation: from the first meeting and discoverycall through to proposal, negotiation, and contract signature across complex, multi-stakeholder deals.• Working closely with technical and product colleagues to shape solutions that address genuine operational challenges for insurance clients, whether in AI, data, or software.• Keeping a close eye on market dynamics, competitor moves, and emerging SaaS trends to continuously refine messaging, pricing, and go-to-market approach.• Keeping the commercial engine running cleanly: CRM records, pipeline accuracy, revenue forecasting, and smooth internal handovers when deals are won.
What You'll Need
• Industry Roots: You have spent meaningful time inside or alongside the US insurance industry and genuinely understand how it operates: the workflows, the compliance pressures, the decision-making structures, and the pain points that technology can actually solve.• A Solid Commercial Track Record: Ideally 3 to 5 years of closing technology deals withinsurance buyers, whether the focus was data platforms, AI/ML solutions, or broader digitalmodernization programs.• A Feel for the InsurTech Space: You follow what is happening across the InsurTechecosystem, you know the major players and the platform dynamics, and you can have aninformed conversation about where the industry is heading.• Experience Navigating Enterprise Buying Processes: Selling into large insuranceorganizations means managing long cycles, multiple stakeholders, and formalprocurement. You have done this before and know how to keep deals moving.• Real Relationships Across the US Insurance Market: You have a network you havebuilt over time and can leverage. And you are skilled at making the case for complextechnology solutions in a way that resonates with both business and technical audiences.
What's on the Table
• Base salary: $110-170K (based on experience)• Bonus, based on jointly agreed KPIs• Sales commission• Health insurance coverage• 25 days PTO
In addition to the compensation structure:• A genuinely senior mandate: you will be the lead commercial voice in the US, with real decision-making authority over how the market is approached.• Direct access to leadership and a senior expert team who are invested in making this work.• The credibility of an established international brand behind you, with a strong delivery record and reference clients in the insurance sector.• Substantive work on real AI and data products for major insurance organizations, not just pitch decks.• No fixed location: work from anywhere in the US.
