# AirGarage — General Manager (West Region)

## Job Description

About AirGarage

AirGarage is on a mission to bring real estate online, starting with parking.

We replace broken parking machines, fragmented software, and manual, labor-intensive operations with a unified, data-rich operating system for parking real estate. We handle everything it takes to run and optimize a parking asset: payments, dynamic pricing, enforcement, license plate recognition, analytics, and more.

By building all of our technology in-house, we are able to deliver a magical experience for drivers while providing real-time visibility and revenue increases of 20-50% or more for real estate owners. That’s why national real‑estate leaders like Hines and Greystar, as well as technology companies like Meta, partner with AirGarage to optimize their parking facilities.

AirGarage uses datapoints like real-time occupancy, local events, weather, driver behavior, competitor pricing, and more to bring true intelligence to real estate owners’ assets for the first time.

We’re investing aggressively to make the physical world legible to a digital system: cameras, sensors, and software that generate a firehose of real-time data about the world around us. That data fuels models and algorithms that allow us to optimize performance at each property while giving owners the clarity and control they’ve never had before.

Before you can optimize an asset, you need to observe it. Before you can observe it, you need to bring it online.

We are starting with the $131 billion US parking real estate market because it is one of the most offline and mismanaged commercial real estate asset types. 26% of the land area in the median American urban core is dedicated to parking.

We are backed by top tier investors including Headline Growth, Andreessen Horowitz, Floodgate, Founders Fund, Abstract Ventures, and angel investors who have founded or worked at companies like Uber, Opendoor, Flexport, and more.

THE ROLE 

As a Regional General Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing day-to-day operations for a portfolio of dozens of parking facilities within your region. Your primary responsibility is to improve the P&L of your region month over month, quarter over quarter, and year over year. You will do this by:

Consistently growing the revenue of each property in your region through pricing, business development, and marketing strategies. Controlling operational expenses for all locations within your region, without sacrificing the quality of our on-the-ground operations. Strengthening owner-partner relationships to retain existing contracts and expand our footprint with each partner. 

In order to succeed, you'll need to be comfortable being both tactical and strategic. You'll need to become an expert on your markets and stay in the weeds to understand the nuances of dozens of unique parking facilities and our various products, while developing processes, improving operational efficiency, and owning a multi-million dollar P&L.

Your day-to-day will require you to wear many hats. Some days you'll be focused on hitting aggressive growth targets, which means developing and implementing pricing, marketing, and business development strategies. Other days you'll be diving deep into property P&Ls, negotiating with service vendors, and reviewing enforcement performance to expand operating margins. And some days you'll be interfacing directly with our owner-partners to report on financial performance, walk through monthly business reviews, and work through the inevitable issues that come up at the property level.

You will manage a Partner Success Manager (who owns the owner-partner relationship, retention, and reporting cadence) and an Operations Manager (who owns field execution, vendor management, and enforcement operations). You'll also work cross-functionally with Sales on new business in your region, with Product on the tools your team relies on, and with Legal on compliance and contract matters.

The ideal candidate will be able to travel up to 25% of the time to visit key owner-partners and properties when required.

What You'll Do 👷‍♀️

Own the regional P&L and top-line growth for your region. Your success will be measured on performance against metrics including revenue growth, owner NOI, and operating margins. Analyze daily, weekly, and monthly performance for each property in your region. Review financial and operational results and implement process improvements based on the findings. Run the owner-partner relationship end-to-end, including monthly business reviews, performance reporting, escalations, and contract renewals. Partner with the VP of Operations to develop the operations strategy roadmap for your region. Build, implement, refine, and manage the day-to-day processes behind a successful parking management operation across AirGarage's national footprint. Develop and implement dynamic pricing, marketing, and business development strategies to drive revenue at portfolio locations. Own enforcement operations across your region, including scan coverage, rescan ratios, citation performance, and the enforcement P&L. Lead operator transitions and new property onboarding, including standardizing prior operator P&Ls to set performance baselines and validate underwriting. Partner with our Legal team to ensure compliance with local city and state governments, and navigate various tax, licensing, and permitting requirements. Implement data-driven solutions to daily operational challenges (price optimization, monthly parker retention, new business generation, and more) to improve the experience of our customers and partners. Work closely with the leadership team and be a key voice in the business to help shape the future of the Operations team at AirGarage.

WHY THIS JOB MAY NOT BE FOR YOU 🚨

Please review this section before applying, we are sharing this information up front because we don’t expect this job to be appealing to everybody.

‘General Manager’ can mean at lot of different things in start ups. At AirGarage it means this: Everything is your responsibility.

This will be a hard job. We work long hours here, including nights and weekends, and you will be expected to do the same. We are growing very fast but our operations team is lean, so every day you will have new challenges and too much on your plate, but you’ll be expected to find a way to deliver anyways.

However, if you are willing to sign up for that, there is a lot to be excited about with this role:

You will learn a ton. You will get exposure to every aspect of the business, going deep in operations, working with Sales, and influencing the future direction of our product. You’ll work hand in hand with a leadership team who will work just as hard as we expect you to. You’ll be a GM on an operations team that we expect to grow significantly over the next 2-3 years, so the potential for future growth is only limited by your own ability & ambition.

If that tradeoff sounds exciting to you, we encourage you to continue reading about the role and would love for you to apply!
