# Ladders — Director, Retail & Strategic Channels

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a Director Retail & Strategic Channels to join the team of a leader in the Manufacturing & Automotive space. This role will lead operational priorities focused on execution quality, efficiency, safety, and measurable performance outcomes. You will collaborate with field leadership, support functions, and cross-functional stakeholders to improve accountability, service delivery, and operational consistency. The position offers the opportunity to influence operational scale, customer outcomes, and organizational performance within a manufacturing environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $161,250 – $236,500 annually

Responsibilities

 Lead strategic initiatives to drive Sound United's growth with national retail partners Develop joint business plans and execute retail strategies Collaborate closely with field teams to enhance partner support Manage inside and outside sales teams focused on national accounts Drive retail execution across multiple channels and teams Foster strong relationships with key stakeholders to ensure alignment and support Implement data-driven approaches for forecasting and business management

Qualifications

 Minimum 10 years of sales experience in North American consumer electronics or related technology categories Minimum 5 years managing national retail accounts, such as Best Buy or other major retailers Proven leadership experience overseeing field and inside sales teams or representative organizations Expertise in developing and executing joint business plans with large retail partners Demonstrated success driving revenue growth and expanding market share Strong analytical skills with a focus on forecasting and financial planning Excellent communication and negotiation skills with senior retail stakeholders

Benefits

 Competitive wellness benefits and 401K retirement savings plan Inclusive and diverse work environment promoting career development Flexible work schedule fostering work-life integration Professional development opportunities through business and leadership academies Tuition reimbursement program for continued education Employee discounts on the company products like JBL and the company/Kardon Recognition and rewards program through 'Be Brilliant'
