# Novitium Energy, A National Energy Partners Company — Business Development and Analytics Manager

## Job Description

ABOUT THE ROLE
Novitium Energy is seeking a driven and analytically rigorous Business Development and Analytics Manager to grow our pipeline of microgrid and distributed energy projects across commercial, industrial, and community markets. This role sits at the intersection of clean energy technology, data analytics, financial modeling, and strategic sales—ideal for a professional who thrives on complexity, loves working with data, and wants to shape the future of resilient energy infrastructure.
You will own the full business development lifecycle for microgrid opportunities—from market prospecting and customer engagement through advanced financial modeling, data-driven proposal development, partner negotiation, and contract execution. You will also lead the development of analytics frameworks that sharpen our market intelligence, improve project economics, and drive better decisions across the BD team. You will work closely with engineering, finance, and operations teams, reporting directly to the VP of Business Development.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
Business Development & Sales:
Identify, develop, and close microgrid project opportunities with C&I, municipal, and utility customers across target marketsLead the full sales cycle from prospecting and qualification through proposal, negotiation, and contract executionBuild and maintain a robust pipeline of distributed energy projects, with disciplined CRM tracking and accurate forecasting.Represent Novitium Energy at industry conferences, utility forums, and customer meetings.
Modeling, Analytics & Data:
Build, maintain, and continuously improve project financial models in Excel, Xendee, Energy Toolbase, and Helioscope to support proposals, investment decisions, and scenario planning.Develop and manage analytics dashboards and datasets that track pipeline performance, market trends, project economics, and BD team KPIs.Conduct load profile analysis, utility bill analysis, and tariff modeling to identify optimal system sizing, dispatch strategies, and customer savings.Perform sensitivity and scenario analyses on key variables—energy prices, incentive levels, demand charge rates, battery degradation—to stress-test project economics.Use data analysis to identify high-value market segments, target customer profiles, and geographic opportunity clusters.Synthesize complex data into clear, compelling visualizations and narratives for customer presentations and executive reporting.
Technical & Financial Expertise:
Scope and conceptually design microgrid systems integrating solar PV, battery energy storage (BESS), backup generation, and advanced controls.Develop project financial models including NPV, IRR, payback, and lifecycle savings analysis, with full sensitivity testing for customer proposals.Evaluate and apply demand response programs, capacity market revenues, utility incentives, and state/federal rebates to optimize project economics.Collaborate with engineering teams to develop technical proposals, single-line diagrams, and preliminary system specifications.
Partnerships, Negotiation & Process:Negotiate commercial terms with technology suppliers, EPC subcontractors, financing partners, and utility counter parties.Structure and manage strategic partnerships with equipment vendors, installers, and co-developers.Drive continuous improvement of BD processes, tools, templates, and playbooks to increase team velocity and win rates.Leverage AI productivity tools and platforms to accelerate research, proposal development, customer outreach, and competitive analysis.
