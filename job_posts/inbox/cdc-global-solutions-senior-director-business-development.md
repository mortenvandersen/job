# CDC Global Solutions — Senior Director Business Development

## Job Description

Director/Sr Director Business Development - Bioanalysis CRO - Northwest
We are proud to be partnering with a leading global bioanalytical and laboratory services organisation supporting pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies across all stages of drug development. 
The company operates 5 laboratories globally with its headquarters based in Pennsylvania, delivering expertise across LC-MS/MS, immunoassays and biomarker services.
This Business Development position will be responsible for generating new business from biotech and pharmaceutical clients, maintaining and growing accounts within territory, and establishing strategic relationships/partnerships with other CROs/CDMOs.
Responsibilities
Drive new business growth and manage key accounts across the assigned territory.Achieve quarterly and annual sales targets, including 20% YOY territory growth.Develop and execute strategic territory and account plans.Build relationships with biotech, pharma, CRO and partner organisations.Identify key decision makers and expand existing customer accounts.Maintain accurate sales activity and pipeline updates within Salesforce CRM.Collaborate cross-functionally with marketing, operations and client services teams.Attend industry conferences, tradeshows and customer meetings.Monitor market trends, competitor activity and emerging opportunities.
Requirements
Bachelor’s degree in a scientific discipline preferred.3+ years’ life sciences sales experience, ideally within bioanalysis or CRO services.Scientific understanding of LC-MS/MS and/or immunoassays preferred.Proven track record of achieving commercial targets and developing strategic accounts.Strong communication, relationship-building and problem-solving skills.Ability to work independently in a field-based, fast-paced environment.Experience using Salesforce CRM preferred.
If interested in this role then apply directly or reach out to harriet@cdcglobal.co.uk
