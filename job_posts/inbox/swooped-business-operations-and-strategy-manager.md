# Swooped — Business Operations and Strategy Manager

## Job Description

Swooped is a Talent Platform (not a staffing agency). We are not the employer for this role and do not make hiring decisions. Clicking "Apply" takes you to Swooped to review the role and hiring company details.
About the OpportunityThe organization is transforming healthcare and improving patient care with an innovative primary care tool. By supporting Primary Care Physicians (PCPs), the organization delivers improved outcomes to patients at a lower cost through early diagnosis and longitudinal care management of chronic conditions.
The company is seeking a high-impact Business Operations and Strategy Manager to help drive its most critical initiatives. This is a pivotal role for an analytical and execution-oriented operator who thrives at the intersection of strategy, operations, and systems design.
This individual will partner closely with the CEO and leadership team to lead high-priority, cross-functional initiatives across the organization. These may span go-to-market expansion, large customer implementations, internal AI adoption, product launches, operational scale-up, or other emerging strategic priorities. The role operates as a deployable problem-solver, bringing structure to ambiguity, driving alignment, and ensuring execution against the organization's most important goals.
This role is ideal for someone who excels at driving clarity and momentum in a fast-paced, evolving environment and is energized by tackling new, complex problems across domains.
Key ResponsibilitiesLead cross-functional strategic initiatives: Own and program-manage high-leverage projects across the organization, which may include commercial expansion, enterprise implementations, AI and technology initiatives, product launches, internal operational transformations, or other emerging priorities.
Serve as a deployable strategic operator: Step into ambiguous or high-stakes situations, diagnose root causes, and design clear execution plans, regardless of function.
Architect scalable systems: Build and refine the operational infrastructure to enable sustainable growth – processes, tools, playbooks, dashboards.
Drive analytical rigor: Collect and analyze business data, generate insights, and present recommendations to leadership to inform strategic decisions.
Build executional rigor: Translate high-level goals into clear plans with workstreams, owners, deliverables, and timelines. Keep the team focused on outcomes.
Align stakeholders across functions: Coordinate across Product, Engineering, Clinical, Commercial, and Operations teams to deliver outcomes—without relying on formal authority.
Translate ambiguity into action: When no playbook exists, build one. When priorities are unclear, force rank them. When coordination breaks down, rebuild the system.
Be a thought partner to leadership: Proactively surface insights, risks, and opportunities; act as an internal consultant to the CEO and leadership team.
Success in this role looks like:First 90 days: Develop a deep understanding of the organization's strategy, product, and operating model. Build strong cross-functional relationships. Take ownership of at least one high-priority initiative and bring structure and forward momentum to it.
First 6 months: Manage a portfolio of strategic initiatives across multiple domains. Implement frameworks for tracking and communicating progress. Lead 1–2 cross-functional efforts to measurable completion and introduce systems that enhance executional clarity.
First year: Meaningfully reduce organizational friction, improve execution velocity across multiple initiatives, and become a trusted, go-to operator for the organization’s most complex challenges.
Required Qualifications5-8 years of experience at a consulting firm, in BizOps at a tech company, or in a high-trust internal strategy/Chief of Staff-type role.
Understanding of value-based care, healthcare operations, and payer/provider dynamics.
Ability to think in structured logic, write with precision, speak in synthesized insights, and distill complex problems into actionable plans.
Experience as a system builder who wants to see the impact of their thinking play out in operations.
Proven track record of driving complex, multi-org initiatives to completion.
Energized by ambiguity, interested in building processes from the ground up, and thriving in a fast-paced environment.
Comfort with shifting priorities and the ability to pivot quickly based on business needs.
Ability to lead without positional authority, earning trust and creating alignment through clarity, quality, and velocity.
