# Sirva — Vice President, Commercial Moving (Virtual, US)

## Job Description

Role OverviewThe Vice President, Commercial Moving is responsible for the overall leadership, strategy, and performance of the Commercial Moving business, including all sales, operations, and project management functions. This role oversees the full commercial moving portfolio, ensuring exceptional service delivery, profitable growth, and operational excellence across all client engagements. The VP leads and develops the team responsible for managing and executing commercial moves, drives the commercial strategy for retaining and expanding the portfolio, and ensures alignment with organizational goals. This position provides strategic oversight across multiple clients and markets, directing resources, setting performance expectations, and guiding the business toward sustained revenue and service growth.

ensure sustainable long‑term solutions.Maintain a deep understanding of client needs, industry requirements, and competitive alternatives.15%Sales Leadership & Revenue ManagementLead the commercial moving sales strategy, including pipeline development, market segmentation, pricing strategy, and revenue forecasting.Oversee strategic contract negotiations, renewals, and pricing decisions to ensure profitable growth.Partner with Sales, Pricing, and Contracts teams to maintain competitive, sustainable margins.Ensure alignment between sales commitments, operational capabilities, and customer expectations.Support the development of new solutions and value propositions that drive commercial growth.10%People Leadership & Organizational DevelopmentLead, develop, and inspire a high‑performing organization across sales, operations, and project management.Establish clear roles, responsibilities, performance expectations, and development plans for all direct and indirect reports.Build leadership capability within the team through coaching, mentorship, and talent management.Drive a culture of accountability, collaboration, safety, and continuous improvement.Oversee workforce planning, succession planning, and organizational design for the Commercial Moving function.10%Business Reviews, Reporting & GovernanceOversee the creation and delivery of business performance reviews, including financial results, sales pipeline, operational performance, customer outcomes, and strategic initiatives.Ensure data‑driven decision‑making through consistent reporting, dashboards, KPIs, and analytics.Lead governance processes for commercial implementations, operational performance, and risk mitigation.Monitor compliance with contractual obligations, service standards, and internal policies.Present regular business updates to the SVP and executive leadership.
