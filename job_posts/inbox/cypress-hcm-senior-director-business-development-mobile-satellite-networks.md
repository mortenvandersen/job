# Cypress HCM — Senior Director Business Development, Mobile & Satellite Networks

## Job Description

Senior Director Business Development, Mobile & Satellite NetworksLocation: Dallas/Reston/Santa Clara (prefer local to office; open to considering remote w/ travel)Employees: ~100Industry: TelecommunicationsReports To: CEO
Seeking a Senior Director Business Development, Mobile & Satellite Networks who is highly experienced and networked to drive commercial growth for advanced Position, Navagation, and Timing (PNT) solutions across global telecom ecosystems. This position will establish and scale strategic partners with high-priority MNO, SNOs, and adjacent connectivity providers.
The ideal candidate will bring deep expertise within the telecom and satellite landscape, a strong executive presence, and a demonstrated ability to structure and close complex partnerships involving network integration, spectrum strategy and mission-critical services.
Most Important Responsibilities:Define and execute partnerships strategies with MNOs, SNOs, and other operator segments to accelerate adoption of advanced PNT and location-based capabilities.Responsible for major MNO/SNO targets and requires consistent ownership to build sustained technical and commercial relationships within major accounts.Drive awareness and interest in PNT solutions and identify fit across MNOs.Build and maintain senior-level relationships across operator organizations, aligning technical, commercial, and strategic priorities.Lead end-to-end deal execution, including opportunity sourcing, business case development, negotiation, and commercialization frameworks for operator-integrated solutions.Articulate the value of 3D positioning and timing technologies to executive, technical, and product stakeholders within operator environments.Drive technical discussions with operator engineering teams, translating network and deployment requirements into product and roadmap inputs.
What you can bring to the table to impact this role, team, and organization:10+ years of experience in enterprise business development, strategic partnerships, or leadership roles within MNOs wireless, satellite, or network infrastructure sectors.Deep network and established relationships within MNO and enterprise telecom.Familiarity with NTN/LEO satellite operations is highly preferred.Proven experience of navigating large telco organizations to reach network, product, and policy teams and structuring commercial partnership frameworks (LOIs, pilot agreements, MoUs).Understanding of 3GPP, 5G Network location services and E911/E-PSAP compliance landscapes strongly preferred.Strong executive communication and comfortability presenting to senior leadership at major MNO companies.Experience managing cross-functional initiatives spanning technical, commercial, and regulatory domains.Executive presence with the ability to engage operators, OEMs, government entities, and investors.Familiarity with 5G, non-terrestrial networks (NTN), network architecture, spectrum strategy, and/or location-based services.Exposure to positioning, navigation, and timing (PNT) technologies preferred.
Compensation: $200 - $233k+ plus 20-30% bonus and equity
