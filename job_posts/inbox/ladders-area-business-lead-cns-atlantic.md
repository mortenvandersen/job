# Ladders — Area Business Lead, CNS - Atlantic

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a Area Business Lead CNS - Atlantic to join the team of a leader in the Retail & Consumer Goods space. This role will lead operational priorities focused on execution quality, efficiency, safety, and measurable performance outcomes. You will collaborate with field leadership, support functions, and cross-functional stakeholders to improve accountability, service delivery, and operational consistency. The position offers the opportunity to influence operational scale, customer outcomes, and organizational performance within a consumer-facing environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $157,700 – $235,750 annually

Responsibilities

 Collaborate with the cross-functional ecosystem team to enhance customer satisfaction and patient outcomes Develop and lead the commercial ecosystem strategy Manage a team of Neuroscience Specialists to drive clinical demand Execute regional plans to meet patient-centric objectives and performance targets Identify and integrate best practices from the healthcare environment into Otsuka's strategies Consolidate insights from various sources to inform business plans Coach team members in crafting effective customer plans based on market dynamics

Qualifications

 Bachelor's degree; MBA or related graduate degree preferred Experience leading account or partnership managers, developing account plans, and contracting with health ecosystem players Cross-functional industry experience in commercial life sciences or related industry engaging with healthcare ecosystem players is advantageous Ability to navigate and thrive in a transformative and ambiguous environment Track record in coaching and mentoring to help achieve goals and responsibilities Proven record of meeting or exceeding quantitative and qualitative targets In-depth knowledge of strategy development, particularly in contracting, is a strong plus Experience in sales management or marketing within Neuroscience or Nephrology market is preferred

Benefits

 Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage Tuition reimbursement and student loan assistance Generous 401(k) match and flexible time off Paid holidays and leave programs Company-provided life insurance and disability coverage

Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
