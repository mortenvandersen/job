# Cadence — Director, M&A Integration

## Job Description

Cadence is seeking an experienced M&A Integration lead to drive integration and value capture for corporate acquisitions. The Director will be tasked with developing plans to integrate and scale the acquired businesses and drive successful execution against key performance metrics supporting the deal. The Group Director will play a critical role in managing cross functional integration teams including Product Marketing and Product Management, R&D, Sales and Sales Operations, Legal, Finance, Operations and HR. This position will report into the Vice President of M&A Integration. This is a highly visible role in the organization and requires frequent engagement and discussions with senior executives including C-suite.
The ideal candidate for this role would be a creative problem solver, an operational leader, and a strong communicator/influencer.Develop comprehensive integration strategy and cross-functional plans for M&A deals informed by the due diligence process and key understanding of the value drivers for the deal and Cadence’s strategyReview transaction rationale and/or deal documents to identify critical business issues and risks, as well as opportunitiesDrive successful execution against plan by tracking milestones and integration risks, making key recommendations to stakeholders, and holding cross functional team accountable for execution of deliverablesEnhance/develop a world class integration framework and processManage and motivate teams of professionals with diverse skills through quantitative and qualitative analysis
Qualifications/Experience:BA / BS degree; MBA or other advanced degree is a plusDemonstrated track record of leading or driving M&A integrations at a consulting firm and/or within Corporate Development or equivalent group – preferably in the software fieldOutstanding problem-solving skills and strong communicator – ability to define and structure problems, develop data driven methodologies, and synthesize recommendations into an executable plan of recordFinancial acumen, acute attention to detail, and a talent for thinking strategicallyEffective organization and time management skills with the ability to work under pressure and adhere to project deadlinesAbility to manage and drive multi-faceted and highly complex integration effortsThe successful candidate will communicate clearly and concisely at the executive level, have strong interpersonal skills, and demonstrate credibility, integrity and trustworthiness in highly confidential situations
