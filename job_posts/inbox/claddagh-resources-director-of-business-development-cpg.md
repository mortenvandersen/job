# Claddagh Resources — Director of Business Development CPG

## Job Description

Business Development Director – CPGUnited States (Remote) | Base salary up to $250,000 + performance bonus
We are hiring on behalf of a global IT services and consulting firm for a senior Business Development Director to join its IoT and Digital Engineering practice. This role drives growth of engineering services across Consumer-Packaged Goods (CPG) clients in North America. You will shape proposals, build and deepen client relationships at executive level, and lead solution-based sales while collaborating with multi-disciplinary teams to create new business opportunities and measurable value for CPG customers.This is a senior, individual-contributor hunter role with a track record requirement of independently scoped and won engineering services deals of $5M+. The successful candidate will bring 13+ years of CPG industry experience and a strong consultative sales DNA.
Key ResponsibilitiesPosition the firm's engineering services value to major CPG customers across Home & Personal Care, Food & Beverages, Alcohol & Tobacco, Beauty & Cosmetics, and Distribution segments in North America.Work as part of a multi-disciplinary team to proactively shape proposals and large deals based on customer requirements.Present, promote, and sell IoT and Engineering services to existing and prospective customers.Establish, develop, and maintain strong relationships with prospects and client team members at executive level.Build trusted client relationships by understanding business priorities and jointly identifying clients' biggest engineering and manufacturing challenges.Collaborate with cross-functional teams to promote engineering, manufacturing, and IoT services and solutions, driving new business opportunities.Carry out market analysis and lead business planning and strategic sales activities; track sales and report status updates.Closely monitor client engagement levels, support business reviews, and implement measures to gain traction with clients.Actively drive awareness of IoT & Engineering solutions with targeted clients through tradeshows, research-led thought leadership, client roadshows and workshops, and social media.
Minimum QualificationsApproximately 13+ years of experience working with CPG customers in professional services and engineering / manufacturing deployments.Familiarity with IT/OT systems used in the industry and with the Purdue model across all levels of a manufacturing facility.Industry experience in R&D, Engineering and Design Services including PLM and Digital Thread solutions.Strong background in problem-solving and solution design, particularly across Digital Engineering, IoT, Cloud, Analytics, and IT/OT.Ability to write specifications based on customer requirements.Direct experience working with CPG customers is mandatory.Domain experience across R&D, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Packaging, Distribution, and Sustainability.Track record of independently scoping and solutioning large deals of $5M+ in engineering, manufacturing services, and solution sales.Track record of consultative, trusted client relationships with strong references built over 5+ years.Demonstrated ability to build and maintain a strong client network within the CPG domain.Experience developing sales plans for a customer portfolio and individual Account Plans.Preferred QualificationsBachelor's or Master's degree in Engineering.
Compensation & BenefitsBase salary up to $250,000, plus performance bonus (eligibility and structure dependent on experience). Comprehensive Employee Benefits package included.Work authorization required; visa sponsorship is not available for this role.
