# Helix — Manager, Strategy and Business Operations

## Job Description

Who We Are & What We Do At Helix, our mission is simple: to help everyone improve their lives through their DNA.
Ready to make a real-world impact with your skills? At Helix, we're transforming healthcare by making genomics a standard of care. We partner with health systems and life science companies to accelerate the integration of genomic data into clinical practice. Join us in building a future where healthcare is personalized, proactive, and powered by genomics.
What is special about this role:The Strategy & Business Operations team is the swiss-army knife of Helix. We help define, scope, and operationalize critical initiatives across the business. As part of a small team that works closely with the leadership team, you will be at the center of the inner workings of how a company makes both strategic and operational decisions to help bring a shared vision to life. You will get your hands dirty across multiple functional areas – from Business Development and Finance, to Product and Science, and more. This is a great opportunity for a self-starter with a strong foundation in healthcare, is energized by the unknown, and thrives in a fast-paced environment.
As a Manager, Strategy & Business Operations you will:Work closely with the leadership team to identify, develop, and implement high-priority, strategic initiativesProvide strategic insight to complex, cross-functional efforts to ensure alignment with business prioritiesLeverage data and business insights to pinpoint operational needs and recommend process improvementsSpearhead the development and execution against detailed project plans to support successful launchesConduct market research to validate hypotheses and identify opportunities to accelerate growthSupport the evaluation of strategic opportunities (e.g. partnerships, acquisitions, etc.)Contribute to the development of Board meeting materials and investor presentationsAct as a key resource in resolving critical and time-sensitive challenges facing the business
About you:BA or BS degree4–6 years of experience in consulting, business operations, corporate strategy, or similar role at a high-growth startup; experience in health tech industry preferredProven track record of delivering results, even when outside past expertiseStrategic, structured problem-solver who can break down ambiguous asks into concrete plansAbility to operate autonomously and influence stakeholders at all levelsCapable of owning the details, while still keeping the big picture in mindAdaptable to shifting priorities, but can bring stability to a dynamic environmentExcellent interpersonal and communication skills, both written and verbalAbility to tell a story focused on insights and data, not just a summary of data aloneHave a knack for learning new things fastHighly proficient with G Suite (Sheets, Docs, Slides), macOS
Pluses:Experience with Tableau, Thoughtspot, HubspotExperience working with health systems or payers
