# Ladders — Corporate Strategy & Diversification Director

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a Corporate Strategy & Diversification Director to join the team of a leader in the Healthcare space. This role will support revenue growth through customer focus, relationship development, and disciplined commercial execution. You will collaborate with sales, customer, product, and operational partners to identify opportunities, solve problems, and improve outcomes. The position offers the opportunity to strengthen market execution, customer relationships, and measurable business results within a healthcare environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $187,000 – $250,000 annually

Responsibilities

 Support the company's regenerative medicine strategy focused on joint repair Conduct landscape assessments, including competitive intelligence and clinical literature reviews Translate scientific insights into evidence-based recommendations for the strategic roadmap Identify and track early-stage investment opportunities and provide portfolio oversight reporting Lead due diligence on investments and partnerships, developing business cases for executive review Evaluate deal frameworks and provide structured analysis for leadership decisions Cultivate relationships with startups, academic institutions, and venture capital firms in the ecosystem Collaborate with cross-functional teams to align on strategic priorities and communicate complex findings

Qualifications

 PhD in relevant life science or medical discipline preferred; strong strategic track record with Master's degree considered 10+ years in strategy, business development, or corporate development in life sciences or MedTech Experience in consulting or business development is valued Bachelor's Degree with 8 years of relevant experience, Associate's Degree with 10 years, or High School Diploma/Equivalent with 12 years of relevant experience acceptable Demonstrated financial literacy and familiarity with venture investment structures

Benefits

 Travel expectations of up to 30% Engagement in industry insights at relevant conferences and forums Opportunity to influence strategic decisions in an emerging field Collaborative work environment with cross-functional teams Exposure to cutting-edge research and development in regenerative medicine

Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
