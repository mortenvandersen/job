# Envision Cold — Director of Business Development

## Job Description

Who we are:Envision Cold is a leading and rapidly growing provider of cold storage and temperature-controlled logistics solutions. We specialize in preserving the integrity of temperature-sensitive products across a diverse range of industries — from food and beverage to pharmaceuticals and beyond. Our full cold-chain capabilities span initial processing through final-mile delivery, supported by a network of best-in-class facilities across North America.
Headquartered in Atlanta, GA, Envision Cold is at an exciting inflection point — expanding our footprint through strategic development, acquisition, and partnerships. We are performance-driven, customer-obsessed, and purpose-built to be the cold storage partner of choice.
What we’re looking for:The Director of Business Development is a senior commercial leader responsible for driving new revenue growth, managing a strategic book of business, and contributing to the financial performance of Envision Cold's facility network. This is a high-impact, externally-facing role that sits at the intersection of strategic sales, customer partnership, and operational ownership.
The ideal candidate is a disciplined, consultative sales executive who leads with curiosity and insight — someone who leverages structured methodologies to navigate complex buying organizations, and close transformational deals. This individual is equally comfortable presenting to a C-suite prospect, partnering with a facility General Manager on P&L performance, and contributing thought leadership that elevates Envision Cold's brand in the market.
This is a fully remote position; however, candidates must reside within a reasonable driving distance of a major airport to support regular nationwide travel. Travel may be required up to 50% based on business needs and could include travel to Mexico and Canada. Candidates must possess a valid passport or be able to obtain one within a reasonable timeframe. Bilingual proficiency in English and Spanish is strongly preferred.
