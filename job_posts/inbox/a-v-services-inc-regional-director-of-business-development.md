# A-V Services, Inc. — Regional Director of Business Development

## Job Description

A-V Services is a premier provider of integrated audiovisual solutions, specializing in high-end design, engineering, and managed services. We empower organizations to collaborate more effectively through cutting-edge AV/IT technology. As we continue our national expansion, we are seeking a high-caliber business development leader to establish and grow our presence in the San Francisco, CA market.
The Role We are looking for a strategic, well-connected Business Development Professional to launch and lead our San Francisco, CA office. This is a unique opportunity for a "player-coach" who possesses a deep roster of active relationships within the Fortune 1000 sector. The ideal candidate will not only understand the nuances of AV/IT integration but will also have the executive presence to navigate complex enterprise environments and secure long-term partnerships.
Key ResponsibilitiesMarket Leadership: Serve as the primary face of A-V Services in the San Francisco, CA region, establishing a local office footprint and driving brand awareness within the technology corridor.Enterprise Growth: Leverage an existing "book of business" and deep-seated industry contacts to secure large-scale AV integration and onsite managed service contracts with Fortune 1000 companies.Strategic Prospecting: Identify and pursue new business opportunities across diverse sectors including enterprise, medical, hospitality, and education.Consultative Selling: Analyze client requirements to provide comprehensive solutions spanning hardware, software, peripherals, and ongoing technical support.Relationship Management: Foster and maintain C-suite and Director-level relationships, ensuring A-V Services is the partner of choice for all collaboration and networking needs.Full Sales Lifecycle: Manage the entire process from initial discovery and technical needs analysis to negotiation and final closing.
Requirements & BehaviorsEstablished Network: A substantial and verifiable track record of success selling to Fortune 1000 clients, with a focus on those headquartered or maintaining major operations in the San Francisco, CA area.Industry Expertise: 5+ years of success in business development specifically within the Audio-Visual or closely related technology integration sector (IT, Networking, or UC).Entrepreneurial Drive: The motivation to run a regional office with a high degree of autonomy and the discipline to manage multiple complex accounts simultaneously.Interpersonal Excellence: Exceptional communication and negotiation skills, with the ability to translate complex technical solutions into clear business value for stakeholders.Adaptability: A keen interest in staying ahead of industry trends, including the latest developments in AI-integrated AV and cloud-based collaboration tools.
What We OfferA financially rewarding career with a highly competitive compensation structure.A comprehensive benefits package.The support of a nationally recognized firm with an outstanding reputation for engineering excellence.An environment committed to professional growth, training, and long-term career advancement.
Full Time Position has benefits including employee stock ownership plan (ESOP), competitive and comprehensive health insurance, life insurance, dental program, 401k, 401k company contribution, short-term and long-term disability insurance, FSA, HRA, Commuter Benefit Card, full paid vacations, and paid holidays.
