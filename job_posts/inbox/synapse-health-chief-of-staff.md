# Synapse Health — Chief of Staff

## Job Description

Who We Are:At Synapse Health, we're streamlining the durable medical equipment (DME) process. We manage intake, documentation, routing, claims, billing, and patient support. Our model reshapes how DME is delivered and experienced.
Since 2016, with decades of industry and leadership experience, we've delivered tech-based solutions that help our partners to modernize operations, improve coordination, and reduce administrative burdens. By taking on operational and financial complexity, we're redefining how DME works for providers, prescribers, and patients. We are proud to offer work that matters, on a mission that matters.
Learn more at SynapseHealth.com and on Synapse Health’s LinkedIn.
What We Need:The Chief of Staff will report directly to the President and serve as a high-impact execution partner, helping to drive cross-functional initiatives, manage leadership workflows, and bring structure to day-to-day operations. This role is focused on creating leverage for the President and Executive Leadership Team (ELT) by ensuring priorities are organized, aligned, and moving forward.
This is a high-visibility opportunity ideal for a high-potential individual who thrives in fast-paced environments and is excited to take ownership, operate with urgency, and build a strong foundation for long-term growth and impact at Synapse.
What You Will Do:Partner closely with the President to manage priorities, coordinate workflows, and ensure effective execution of key initiativesOwn ELT agendas, meeting preparation, and follow-ups to drive alignment and accountability across leadershipOwn and drive OKR, MBR, and planning cadences by coordinating inputs, preparing stakeholders, and ensuring accountability and follow-through across teamsDrive cross-functional initiatives and special projects from ambiguity to execution, ensuring progress and clear outcomesPlan and support leadership offsites and key internal meetings to improve organizational effectivenessAct as a connector across teams, ensuring stakeholders are aligned and workstreams are moving forwardIdentify gaps, remove blockers, and proactively step in to support business needs with urgency and resourcefulnessSupport internal communications and preparation of materials for leadership discussionsExplore, test, and drive adoption of AI tools and internal workflows to improve efficiency across the organizationAssist with analytical work such as tracking progress, synthesizing insights, and supporting decision-making as needed
What You Have: At Synapse Health we’ve intentionally built a culture of kindness, collaboration and creativity, and we consider those qualities to be table stakes for any new team member. Additional requirements include:6-10+ years of experience in consulting, business operations, corporate development, business development, or similar roles in fast-paced or high-growth environmentsExceptional written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to build strong relationships across all levels of the organizationDemonstrated ability to operate in ambiguity, bring structure to complex situations, and drive workstreams forwardExperience supporting or driving OKR, MBR, or similar business review cadences, with a focus on coordination, stakeholder preparation, and accountabilityStrong organizational skills and attention to detail, with experience managing multiple priorities, agendas, and follow-upsProven ability to influence without authority and collaborate effectively across cross-functional teamsHigh ownership mindset with a strong sense of urgency and proactive approach to problem-solvingInterest in leveraging AI tools and technology to improve workflows and drive efficiencyStrong analytical thinking skills, with proficiency in Excel/Google Sheets and presentation toolsDemonstrated ability to take initiative, drive work independently, and follow through without requiring constant direction in a fast-paced, evolving environmentBachelor’s degree required. What Sets You Apart:You’re highly organized and take pride in keeping people, projects, and priorities alignedYou see around corners and think two steps aheadYou’re a problem-solver who brings solutions, not just questionsYou have a low ego and are willing to roll up your sleeves to do whatever the business needsYou build strong relationships and know how to influence without authorityYou’re curious, proactive, and eager to experiment with new tools, including AIYou thrive in fast-paced environments and are motivated by ownership, impact, and growthYou want to grow into a senior operator within the company
