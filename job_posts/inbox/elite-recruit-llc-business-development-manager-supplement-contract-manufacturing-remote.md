# Elite Recruit LLC — Business Development Manager - Supplement Contract Manufacturing - Remote

## Job Description

Elite Recruit has partnered with an innovative supplement contract manufacturer (CDMO) in the hunt for a dynamic Business Development Manager (US) with a proven track record in dietary supplements business development, strategic planning, and cross-functional collaboration. This role is pivotal in driving multi-channel sales and market share growth in alignment with the company’s strategic initiatives
Responsibilities:
As the Business Development Manager, you’ll be the key to unlocking new opportunities. From acquiring B2B customers from the USA and internationally to fostering long-lasting relationships, your role will be pivotal in shaping the future of the company.
Your deep knowledge of the industry and a keen eye on market trends will give you the edge in staying ahead of the competition.
You’ll be the connector between clients and internal teams, ensuring seamless execution of projects while developing creative proposals that meet their manufacturing needs.
Your sales strategy will not just meet targets—it will exceed them, powered by your extensive industry network and unmatched negotiation skills.

Requirements:
Education: Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or a related field.
Technical Knowledge: Strong familiarity with product formulation and manufacturing technologies in the nutritional supplement industry.
Experience: A minimum of 3-5 years of experience in business development and sales within the dietary supplement contract manufacturing industry.
B2B Expertise: Proven success in acquiring and managing B2B clients supplement manufacturing space.
Industry Knowledge: Comprehensive understanding of contract manufacturing processes, including regulatory compliance and production timelines in the nutritional supplement space.
