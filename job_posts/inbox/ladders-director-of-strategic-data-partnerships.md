# Ladders — Director of Strategic Data Partnerships

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a Director Of Strategic Data Partnerships to join the team of a leader in the Information Technology space. This role will turn data into practical insight that improves visibility, decision-making, and business performance. You will partner with business, technical, and operational stakeholders to build reporting, surface trends, and translate analysis into action. The position offers the opportunity to strengthen data-driven execution and help teams focus on the metrics that matter most within a technology-driven environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $350,000 – $350,000 annually

Responsibilities

 Identify and evaluate potential high-value data partners Engage with CEOs and senior executives to assess partnership opportunities Lead diligence processes to understand data quality and long-term applicability Develop strategic business cases for partnership Negotiate complex, enterprise-level agreements Coordinate with cross-functional teams including legal and product Prioritize opportunities and make strategic decisions about pursuit

Qualifications

 Director-level experience in enterprise sales, strategic partnerships, corporate development, investment banking, or strategy consulting Comfortable engaging with and influencing C-suite executives Experience in evaluating and executing complex, multi-stakeholder transactions Ability to thrive in less structured environments and carve new paths Demonstrated grit and resilience in long-cycle negotiations

Benefits

 Flexible work hours and an open vacation policy Generous 401(k) match and parental leave Wellness and work-from-home benefits Learning and professional development support

Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
