Business Development Director - 
Are you a commercially driven leader with a passion for building meaningful partnerships in the healthcare and life sciences space?
We are seeking an experienced Business Development Director to play a key role in driving the next phase of growth within a dynamic and forward-thinking medical communications environment. This is a strategic, high-impact position where you’ll be responsible for identifying new opportunities, shaping growth strategies, and developing long-term partnerships with leading pharmaceutical and healthcare clients.
As a Business Development Director, you will lead the full lifecycle of new business development. From identifying opportunities and initiating client conversations to leading pitches and securing major accounts. Working closely with senior internal teams, you’ll help transform market insight into winning proposals and differentiated solutions.
In this role, you will lead the development of new client partnerships while helping existing accounts grow and evolve. You’ll combine market insight, strategic thinking, and strong relationship-building skills to create a robust pipeline of opportunities and lead high-impact pitch processes.
Working alongside senior experts across scientific, strategic, and client services teams, you’ll help develop differentiated solutions that address complex client challenges across areas such as real world evidence, publication planning, medical affairs, medical writing, or full-service medical communications.
You will also represent the organisation externally, building credibility across the industry, engaging with senior decision-makers, and positioning the team as a trusted strategic partner.
