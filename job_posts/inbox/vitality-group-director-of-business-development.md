# Vitality Group — Director of Business Development

## Job Description

We are looking for a Director of Business Development to help drive growth and strategic relationships across the Education sector in the San Francisco Bay Area. This is a high-visibility role focused on developing long-term client relationships, identifying new opportunities, and helping position the company for continued growth across K-12, Higher Education, and institutional projects. We are looking for someone who understands the market, communicates well with ownership groups and industry partners, and can help continue growing the company’s presence throughout the region.
Qualifications• 10+ years of experience preferably with a Top ENR GC• Experience in commercial construction with a strong focus on Education projects• Proven ability to develop clients, generate opportunities, and support successful project pursuits• Strong communication, presentation, and relationship-building skills• Self-motivated with the ability to operate effectively in a high-autonomy environment
This opportunity offers a strong platform for someone who wants to play a key role in long-term regional growth within a respected and established contractor. The culture is grounded in respect, reliability, and accountability. Compensation is aligned, including base salary, performance-based incentives tied to branch results, and a comprehensive benefits package.
If interested, please apply. You are also welcome to connect with one of our associates if you do not have an updated resume, or visit our website and complete the short form so we can schedule a time to discuss further. All inquiries are confidential.
