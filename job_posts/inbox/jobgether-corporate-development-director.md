# Jobgether — Corporate Development Director

## Job Description

This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for a Corporate Development Director in India.

This is a high-impact strategic leadership role focused on driving inorganic growth, partnerships, and investment initiatives within a deep-tech and defense-focused organization. You will operate at the intersection of corporate strategy, M&A, and executive decision-making, shaping long-term growth priorities in highly complex and regulated markets. The role involves leading end-to-end deal execution while also identifying new strategic opportunities across defense, autonomy, and AI ecosystems. You will work closely with senior leadership to translate ambiguous strategic questions into structured, data-driven recommendations that inform capital allocation and expansion strategies. The environment is fast-paced, highly technical, and mission-driven, requiring strong judgment and autonomy. This is a pivotal role for someone who thrives in high-stakes deal-making and strategic advisory at scale.

Originate, evaluate, and lead strategic partnerships that strengthen market position and accelerate technological and commercial growthStructure, negotiate, and execute complex agreements including joint ventures, alliances, and long-term strategic collaborationsLead end-to-end M&A and investment processes, including sourcing, valuation, financial modeling, due diligence, negotiation, and integration planningDevelop and present high-quality investment and transaction materials for internal and external stakeholders, including executive leadership and boardsBuild and maintain senior-level relationships across industry, government, and strategic ecosystem partners to identify new growth opportunitiesServe as a strategic advisor to executive leadership on corporate development, capital allocation, and inorganic growth strategyDrive cross-functional execution across finance, legal, product, engineering, and operations to ensure successful deal delivery and integrationConduct market intelligence analysis across defense, AI, and autonomy sectors to identify risks, opportunities, and competitive dynamics

Requirements
