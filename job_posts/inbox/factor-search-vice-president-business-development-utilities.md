# Factor Search — Vice President Business Development - Utilities

## Job Description

The Company
We are currently partnered with a leader in energy intelligent solutions and microgrid technology. Helping businesses, utilities, and communities unlock the full value of their assets - reducing costs and accelerating the transition to a future of sustainable energy.
The Opportunity
We are currently seeking a high-impact Vice President of Business Development to spearhead business development activities across the US and international markets. Reporting directly to the President, this is a senior, autonomous role - someone who can identify and develop new commercial opportunities, expand client presence in various geographies, and create strategic partnerships that drive durable revenue growth.
This role sits outside the core sales organisation, offering a unique mandate to develop and execute growth initiatives with direct executive visibility. You will work closely with leadership and help shape new verticals, markets and customer segments.
Key Responsibilities
Market Expansion & GTM Strategy
Identify, evaluate, and pursue new business opportunities with a specific focus on geographies where they currently have limited client presence.Develop market entry strategies for priority international regions, accounting for regulatory landscape, competitive dynamics, and local partnership needs.Define and own an organic growth pipeline - distinct from M&A and corporate development activities - that directly contributes to revenue trajectory.Serve as a forward-looking strategist, anticipating shifts in energy markets and positions ahead of competitive change.
Client Development & PartnershipsBuild and cultivate senior-level relationships with potential clients, strategic partners, channel partners, and industry stakeholders across target markets.Lead the development of new client accounts from initial outreach through to commercial engagement, partnering with the Sales team on handoff and closure.
Executive Leadership & Cross-Functional CollaborationReport directly to the President and serve as a key voice on commercial strategy, contributing to executive-level planning and decision-making.Partner cross-functionally with Sales, Marketing, Product, and Finance teams to ensure business development initiatives are well-resourced and commercially sound.Collaborate cross-functionally and align between organic growth efforts and M&A / strategic investment activities.
Professional Experience10+ years of business development, commercial strategy or strategic partnerships within energy, cleantech, utilities, or an adjacent technology sector.Proven track record of opening new markets and generating organic revenue growth, not just managing existing accounts.Experience operating across international markets; familiarity with energy markets outside the US is a strong advantage.Background working in energy storage, distributed energy resources (DER), microgrids, or utility-scale renewable energy is highly preferred.
Skills & AttributesA natural networker and relationship builder who opens doors through credibility, not just persistence.Strategic thinker with strong commercial acumen - able to assess market opportunities rigorously and build compelling business cases.Excellent communicator and executive presence; comfortable presenting to C-suite stakeholders both internally and externally.Highly self-directed with an entrepreneurial mindset - this role rewards initiative and original thinking.
