# Swooped — Chief of Staff, R&D

## Job Description

Swooped is a Talent Platform (not a staffing agency). We are not the employer for this role and do not make hiring decisions. Clicking "Apply" takes you to Swooped to review the role and hiring company details.
About the OpportunityReady to be a Titan? The Chief of Staff, R&D is a high-impact, strategic partner embedded at the heart of the Research & Development organization. This role is a force multiplier for the Chief Technology and Product Officer, and the broader R&D leadership team - accelerating decision-making, driving operational excellence, and ensuring the organization is focused on the right problems at the right time.
This is not a support role. It's a seat at the table. The Chief of Staff, R&D acts as an extension of the CTPO - helping shape priorities, orchestrate cross-functional work, and translate strategy into execution across Engineering, Product, Design, Data, and Information Security.
The R&D org is the engine of the company - it builds the platform that powers the trades, an industry that keeps homes running and businesses growing. This role sits at the center of that engine, with visibility into everything from long-range product strategy to how talent is hired and developed. If you want scope, impact, and the opportunity to help shape one of the most important organizations at the company - this is it.
Key ResponsibilitiesStrategic Partner to the Head of R&D: Serve as a thought partner and trusted advisor to the Head of R&D - helping shape priorities, prepare for key decisions, and drive alignment across the organization. Represent R&D leadership in cross-functional forums, escalations, and strategic initiatives as needed. Proactively surface high-impact opportunities to improve business processes across both the R&D org and the company as a whole.
R&D Operating Rhythm & Execution: Own the operational cadence of the R&D org: leadership team meetings, quarterly planning cycles, OKR tracking, roadmap reviews, and all-hands. Design and continuously improve the processes that keep teams aligned, accountable, and unblocked. Ensure decisions are captured, owners are assigned, and follow-through actually happens.
Cross-Functional Connector: Act as the primary connective tissue between R&D and the rest of the business - Finance, People Ops, GTM, and Corporate Engineering. Partner with other Chiefs of Staff and functional leaders to ensure cross-organizational alignment. Facilitate cross-functional initiatives, surface dependencies early, and ensure R&D's priorities and progress are clearly communicated company-wide.
Insights & Business Health: Maintain clear dashboards and narrative updates so R&D leaders see progress, risks, and trade-offs in one place. Stay on top of the metrics that matter most: engineering velocity, product delivery, headcount and capacity, and investment allocation. Translate data into clear, actionable recommendations for leadership.
Strategic Programs & Special Projects: Lead high-priority, often ambiguous initiatives that don't fit neatly into an existing team's charter - from org design to M&A diligence support to new capability buildout. Operate at a tactical, strategic, and operational level, handling oversight of projects that fall between departments or leadership areas of responsibility.
Communications & Executive Presence: Draft and own key R&D communications including executive memos, board and investor materials, all-hands content, and internal updates. Ensure the Head of R&D is well-prepared for every meeting - with the right context, pre-reads, and follow-up. Maintain transparency and alignment across the organization.
Talent & Culture Champion: Partner with People Ops and R&D leadership on hiring strategy, onboarding, performance cycles, and culture initiatives. Help build the high-emotional-intelligence environment where great engineers and product builders do the best work of their careers.
Required Qualifications8+ years of experience in strategy, operations, management consulting, or a Chief of Staff / Senior Program Management role within a high-growth SaaS or technology company
At least 3 years in a Chief of Staff, Senior Program Manager, or equivalent strategic operational role working directly with VP-level or above leadership
Deep fluency in how R&D organizations work - understanding the product development lifecycle, engineering org structures, and the relationship between roadmap, resourcing, and business outcomes
Strong analytical and financial acumen - ability to build a model, read a P&L, and translate numbers into a story that drives decisions
Exceptional communication and executive presence - ability to write clearly, present confidently, and tailor a message for various audiences
A pragmatic driver mentality - ability to dig into technical details, thoughtfully question approaches for efficiency, and move quickly without sacrificing quality
Operational horsepower - passion for building process where there is none, and simplifying process where there's too much; passion for scalable systems in a high-growth, constantly changing environment
High emotional intelligence - ability to build strong, trusted relationships across all levels of an organization, and earn influence, not authority
Comfort with ambiguity - seamless shift between strategic planning and operational problem-solving, and ability to bring calm to fast-moving, complex environments
Bachelor's degree required in Engineering, Computer Science, Business, or related field
Preferred QualificationsB2B SaaS experience strongly preferredFamiliarity with program/project management methodologies (Agile, PMP, CSM) a plusMBA or advanced degree preferred
