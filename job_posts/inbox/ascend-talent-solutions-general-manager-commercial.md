# Ascend Talent Solutions — General Manager Commercial

## Job Description

Our client is one of the largest Commercial Real Estate and Property Management companies in California managing commercial real estate in Northern California focused on converting and increasing the value of well-located office and industrial properties needs a strong, financial minded General Manager for a multi-tenant property with active facilities and space management.
Job Description – The General Manager will oversee all aspects of the property management team, leading and implementing customized asset strategies to grow the financial performance of the portfolio. Managing on-site property management team, reporting on the portfolio financial performance, ensuring the fiscal and operational success of the properties. With excellent financial analysis skills, the primary responsibility of the General Manager will be the financial management and reporting for the portfolio of properties working closely with the Property Management team to drive improved performance and value creation. With a professional and collaborative manner, the General Manager will work with property managers to support the portfolio growth and retention goals. Strong accountability and analytics are key attributes of the successful General Manager. Additional responsibilities for the General Manager include the following:
