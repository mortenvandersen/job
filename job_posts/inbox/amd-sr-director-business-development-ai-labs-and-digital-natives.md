THE ROLE

AMD is seeking a Senior Director of Business Development to lead global strategy and revenue growth across AI labs and model providers within the Data Center GPU Business Unit. This executive will define and execute AMD’s engagement model for AI-native companies, foundation model developers, AI super users, and large-scale digital native enterprises building advanced AI capabilities.

This leader will be responsible for driving large, strategic platform engagements that accelerate adoption of AMD Instinct™ accelerators and AMD’s AI software ecosystem across frontier AI workloads, large-scale model training, inference platforms, and next-generation AI infrastructure deployments.

The role requires a blend of executive-level relationship leadership, AI ecosystem fluency, commercial rigor, and cross-functional influence to scale AMD’s position in one of the most competitive and strategically important markets in technology.

THE PERSON

The ideal candidate brings deep knowledge of AI infrastructure, foundation model workloads, and accelerated compute platforms, along with a proven ability to build and scale strategic relationships in fast-moving markets.

You Demonstrate

Executive presence with the ability to influence at the board and C-suite levelExperience leading large-scale platform negotiationsStrong understanding of AI model training economics and inference scalingAbility to translate complex technical capabilities into strategic business valueA track record of driving significant revenue growth in highly competitive environment


Key Responsibilities

Own global business development strategy for AI end users within the DC GPU Business Unit.Drive significant revenue growth through multi-year, platform-level engagements.Develop and execute account strategies across AI-native startups, frontier model developers, and digital-first enterprises.Structure and close complex commercial agreements involving hardware, software, and ecosystem components.


Executive Engagement & Industry Presence

Establish and maintain C-level relationships with AI and digital enterprise leaders.Position AMD as a long-term AI platform partner across training and inference workloads.Represent AMD in strategic industry discussions related to AI infrastructure scaling.


Platform & Ecosystem Development
