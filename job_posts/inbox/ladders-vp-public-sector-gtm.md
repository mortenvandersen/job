# Ladders — VP, Public Sector GTM

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a VP Public Sector GTM to join the team of a leader in the Information Technology space. This role will lead operational priorities focused on service delivery, efficiency, quality, and day-to-day execution. You will partner with field leadership, customer-facing teams, and support functions to improve performance, solve operating challenges, and maintain accountability. The position offers the opportunity to influence operational scale, team development, and measurable outcomes in an execution-focused environment within a technology-driven environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $263,000 – $447,000 annually

Responsibilities

 Define and execute the growth strategy for the Global Public Sector business Lead and inspire a global go-to-market team through effective coaching and strategic activation Engage with executive-level customers to drive action using AI and platform capabilities Champion key accounts by collaborating with customers and internal teams for integrated solutions Establish structured rhythms for data-driven decision-making and execution excellence Harness analytics to identify trends and design initiatives for growth acceleration Cultivate partnerships with consultancies and advisory practices to enhance market positioning

Qualifications

 Bachelor's Degree in Public Administration, International Relations, or Business Administration; Master's Degree preferred 15+ years of leadership experience in the Public Sector or enterprise technology Proven transformational impact in Public Sector operations and missions 10+ years of experience in leading global teams Expertise in applying technology and AI for operational efficiency and transformation Strong executive presence to engage with senior leaders and build strategic relationships Analytical mindset with data-driven decision-making skills

Benefits

 Health plans including flexible spending accounts and a 401(k) Plan with company match Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP) and matching donations Flexible time away plan and family leave programs

Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
