# VytlOne — Vice President, Rare and Orphan Specialty Market Development

## Job Description

Overview

 Vice President, Rare and Orphan Specialty Market Development - VytlOne

The Vice President, Commercial Specialty Market Development is responsible for identifying, developing, and scaling strategic growth opportunities within the rare and orphan targeted specialty markets. This executive leader drives market expansion by building go-to-market strategies, forming strategic partnerships, and aligning cross-functional teams to deliver innovative solutions that meet the unique needs of the rare and orphan specialty customer segments. The role requires a combination of strategic vision, deep market insight, and strong commercial execution to accelerate revenue growth, expand market share, and position the company as a leader in rare and orphan market segments.

Core Values

At VytlOne, our success is guided by our core values: Authenticity, Excellence for Partners, Integrity, One Team, and Purpose. The VP, Commercial Specialty Market Development will exemplify these values while driving strategic growth and building strong partnerships across the specialty markets.

Responsibilities

 ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS (These duties must be performed with or without reasonable accommodation. This document in no way states or implies that these are the only duties to be performed by the employee occupying this position): 

 Strategic Rare and Orphan Drug Market Development 

 Identify and prioritize high-value rare and orphan specialty drug markets aligned with the company’s long-term growth strategy.  Develop and execute market entry and expansion strategies for new and emerging segments.  Conduct market analysis to understand customer needs, competitive landscape, regulatory considerations, and growth potential.  Build multi-year strategic plans to capture opportunities within specialty verticals. 

 Commercial Strategy & Revenue Growth in the Rare and Orphan Specialty Drug Market 

 Design and implement go-to-market strategies tailored to specialty market customers.  Partner with Sales, Marketing, and Product teams to build offerings that meet segment-specific requirements.  Drive pipeline generation and revenue growth across targeted markets.  Establish pricing, positioning, and value propositions that resonate with specialty market buyers. 

 Cross-Functional Leadership 

 Lead cross-functional collaboration between Sales, Marketing, Product Development, Operations, and Finance.  Translate market insights into actionable product, service, and commercialization strategies.  Ensure alignment between corporate strategy and specialty market initiatives. 

 Strategic Partnerships & Ecosystem Development 

 Identify and develop strategic partnerships, alliances, and channel relationships to accelerate market penetration.  Represent the organization with key industry groups, partners, and customers.  Negotiate and structure partnership agreements that create long-term strategic value. 

 Rare and Orphan Pharmacy Customer & Market Intelligence 

 Develop deep relationships with key customers and industry stakeholders in specialty markets.  Monitor industry trends, regulatory developments, and competitive activities.  Translate insights into strategic recommendations for executive leadership. 

 Performance Management 

 Establish KPIs and metrics to track rare and orphan drug market development performance.  Manage budgets and resource allocation to maximize ROI.  Deliver regular executive updates on progress, opportunities, and risks.
