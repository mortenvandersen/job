# 3 Bridge Networks — Vice President Finance

## Job Description

We are partnering with a fast-growing investment firm operating at the intersection of private equity and venture capital to hire a VP of Finance. This is a highly visible leadership role that will work closely with the firm’s partners and investment team and play a critical role in building and scaling the finance infrastructure as the platform continues to grow.
The firm has developed an excellent reputation in the market with a strong track record of performance and successful fundraising. They are known for maintaining a collaborative and thoughtful culture where team members have meaningful exposure to leadership and strategic decision-making. This role will have direct interaction with partners, investors, and key external stakeholders.
Key Responsibilities• Oversee the finance and accounting operations for both the management company and investment funds• Lead the preparation and review of quarterly and annual financial reporting, including coordination with third-party fund administrators• Manage budgeting, forecasting, and cash management for the management company and support firm-level financial planning• Serve as a key liaison with auditors, tax advisors, fund administrators, and other external service providers• Support fundraising and investor relations efforts, including LP reporting, diligence requests, and investor communications• Partner closely with the investment team to provide financial insights related to firm performance, fund economics, and strategic initiatives• Help design and implement scalable finance processes, internal controls, and reporting systems as the firm continues to grow• Lead and mentor members of the finance and accounting team, helping to develop a best-in-class finance function• Assist with strategic initiatives, including new fund launches, operational improvements, and firm-level growth projects
Ideal Background• 10+ years of experience in finance or accounting within private equity, venture capital, or alternative asset management• Strong understanding of fund structures, management company accounting, carried interest, and LP reporting• Experience working closely with investment partners and senior leadership in a dynamic environment• Prior experience managing relationships with fund administrators, auditors, and tax providers• CPA strongly preferred but not required• Comfortable operating in a high-growth, entrepreneurial environment where processes continue to evolve• Strong communication skills with the ability to interact with both internal leadership and external investors
