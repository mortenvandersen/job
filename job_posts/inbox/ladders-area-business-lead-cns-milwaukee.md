# Ladders — Area Business Lead, CNS - Milwaukee

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a Area Business Lead CNS - Milwaukee to join the team of a leader in the Retail & Consumer Goods space. This role will lead continuous improvement work focused on process discipline, productivity, and measurable operating gains. You will partner with operational leaders and project stakeholders to standardize workflows, identify bottlenecks, and improve execution quality. The position offers the opportunity to create measurable efficiency gains and scalable operational improvements within a consumer-facing environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $157,700 – $235,750 annually

Responsibilities

 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to enhance customer satisfaction and improve patient outcomes Drive the ecosystem commercial strategy and execute regional plans to achieve performance targets Lead a team of Neuroscience Specialists to stimulate clinical demand and improve customer experiences Identify and integrate best practices from the healthcare ecosystem into Otsuka's strategies Build and leverage a network of relationships within the healthcare ecosystem to understand customer needs Engage with senior customers to foster strategic partnerships centered on patient outcomes Foster a collaborative, patient-centric culture while tracking customer experiences for continuous improvement

Qualifications

 Bachelor's degree; MBA or other related graduate degree preferred Experience leading account or partnership managers with a focus on health ecosystems Cross-functional experience in commercial life sciences (pharma/biotech) or related fields Ability to navigate an ambiguous and transforming environment Track record in coaching, training, and mentoring peers toward achieving their goals Proven success in meeting or exceeding financial and qualitative targets Strong understanding of healthcare compliance and regulatory challenges

Benefits

 Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage Life, accidental death, and disability insurance Tuition reimbursement and student loan assistance Generous 401(k) match and flexible time off Paid holidays and leave programs
