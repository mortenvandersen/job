# Merraine Group — General Manager / Single-Threaded Owner (STO)

## Job Description

A mission-driven organization operating at the intersection of technology, media, research, and philanthropy is seeking a General Manager / Single-Threaded Owner (STO) to design and execute a major initiative focused on narrative dynamics within the American information ecosystem.This role is built for a high-agency operator who can translate strategy into action. The GM will own a portfolio of initiatives aimed at identifying and influencing high-impact narratives, mobilizing credible messengers, and strengthening the ecosystem’s ability to respond to antisemitism, anti-Western ideology, misinformation, manipulation, and coordinated influence campaigns.
You will operate as the accountable owner, setting direction, building systems, and delivering measurable outcomes.
About the OrganizationThe organization is a new initiative backed by leading technologists, business leaders, and philanthropists working to counter the growing influence of antisemitism, anti-Western ideology, and coordinated misinformation.
While many organizations are working in this space, most were built for a slower, less adversarial information environment. This initiative exists to modernize how these challenges are addressed—equipping the ecosystem with the data, tools, partnerships, and strategic capabilities required to respond to rapidly evolving narratives, including those that undermine democratic values and target Jewish communities.
The organization combines the mission focus of a nonprofit with the agility of a startup and is led by operators from technology, media, finance, and growth-stage companies.
What You’ll OwnYou will design and run a comprehensive initiative that:Identifies high-leverage narrative opportunities, particularly those shaping perceptions around antisemitism and Western institutionsDevelops messaging that resonates with key audiences and counters harmful ideological narrativesBuilds and activates networks of creators, influencers, and trusted messengersDetects and exposes coordinated, foreign-driven, or ideologically motivated influence operationsBuilds real-time narrative intelligence and research capabilitiesPartners with media outlets, platforms, and institutions to shape accurate, values-aligned narrativesDeploys rapid-response efforts during major events or narrative inflection points
Key ResponsibilitiesServe as Single-Threaded Owner of a strategic initiative from concept through executionDevelop narrative strategies and campaign frameworks addressing antisemitism, anti-Western sentiment, and misinformationRecruit and activate creators, researchers, and ecosystem partnersBuild systems for data, monitoring, and real-time insightsCoordinate rapid-response messaging during major narrative shifts or crisesEstablish clear metrics, accountability, and operational discipline
Ideal BackgroundStrong candidates may come from technology, media, startups, communications, policy, or public-interest organizations and will likely bring experience in areas such as:Strategy and operations leadershipNarrative strategy or communications campaignsDigital media ecosystems and creator networksCounter-misinformation, extremism, or influence analysisData-driven decision making
Core TraitsOwner mentality - thrives with full accountabilitySpeed and execution - moves quickly from idea to actionStrategic judgment - understands narrative, cultural, and ideological dynamicsOperational rigor - builds systems that scaleNetwork builder - able to mobilize partners and creators
CultureThe team operates with a flat, collaborative, and execution-focused culture. Everyone contributes to both strategy and implementation. Strong ideas are valued, but success is measured by the ability to translate those ideas into tangible outcomes and real-world impact.
