About The Job

Google's Brand and Reputation team is a cross-functional leadership forum mandated with driving positive outcomes on complex, sensitive issues in the US market, aimed at enhancing Google's brand and reputation. The forum is composed of Senior US Leaders across Global Business, Marketing, Government Affairs & Public Policy (GAPP), Communications, Partnerships and others.

As the strategy and operations lead for Google Brand and Reputation, you will partner with the forum chair (President of ALCS) to enable the outcomes of brand and reputation initiatives with Key Opinion Formers (KOFs): policy makers, press, partners, and the general public. Specifically, in this role you will drive brand and reputation forum business cadence and enable progress against strategic priorities.

The Go-to-Market Operations (GtM) team ensures Google's complex and ever-evolving Ads business runs smoothly. We are instrumental in setting go-to-market strategy, and ensuring flawless execution and operations against the strategy. We have teams embedded in each of the major Ads business areas as well as global teams that work across the business areas. Team members are analytical and strategic, with a pragmatic sense of how to get things done.
