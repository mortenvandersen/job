# Ladders — Commercial Territory Partner-Led GTM

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a Commercial Territory Partner-led GTM to join the team of a leader in the Information Technology space. This role will drive marketing initiatives focused on growth, customer acquisition, and measurable demand creation. You will collaborate with analytics, product, creative, and commercial teams to turn market insights into scalable campaigns and performance improvements. The position offers the opportunity to directly influence customer engagement, conversion performance, and revenue growth within a technology-driven environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $172,020 – $275,360 annually

 Lead strategic initiatives for pipeline growth through partner engagement Establish consistent operating practices across regions and segments Facilitate collaboration by sharing best practices from partner-led activities Develop dashboards and reports to drive KPI visibility Collaborate with teams to ensure accurate reporting for partner activities Conduct structured business performance reviews for leadership Oversee Territory Management processes and account hygiene

 5-7 years of experience in partner engagement and sales strategy execution Exceptional leadership and communication skills for influencing diverse stakeholders Strong project management capabilities with a focus on measurable outcomes Track record of executing programs that drive partner performance Established ability to build long-term relationships with internal and external stakeholders Experience in selling software or infrastructure solutions to mid-market or enterprise accounts Preferred knowledge of the company’s Partner Ecosystem and Programs

Benefits

 Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage Flexible Spending Account for healthcare and dependent care 401(k) retirement plan with employer match Paid time off alongside holidays Paid parental leave for new parents Various leave benefits including disability and military leave Employee stock purchase plan and tuition reimbursement
