# Step Ahead Recruitment Ltd — Director of Business Development & Partnerships

## Job Description

Director of Business Development & Strategic PartnershipsRedwood City, California | HybridUp to $212,000 base salary + cash incentive plan
A growing lifestyle brand is seeking a senior strategic partnerships professional to build and scale high-impact national partnerships across hospitality, retail, restaurant groups, and experiential venues.
This is a hands-on, entrepreneurial role for someone who thrives in a business development environment, enjoys opening doors at enterprise level, and can build long-term commercial partnerships from strategy and outreach through to execution and optimization.
What’s in it for you?• Competitive base salary up to $212,000 with annual salary reviews• Cash incentive plan• Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage• Generous parental leave program• Company-paid life and AD&D insurance• Short and long-term disability insurance• 401(k)• Employee Assistance Program (EAP) and wellness perks• Paid holidays, annual shutdown week, and volunteer time off• Sabbatical opportunities after long-term service• Continuous learning benefits• Flexible work-from-home setup reimbursement• Merchandise discounts• And much more!
What you’ll do:• Develop and execute partnership strategies aligned with company growth goals across retail, hospitality, food & beverage, and experiential venues• Identify, pitch, and secure national and multi-location partnerships that drive revenue, brand awareness, and customer acquisition• Own partnerships end-to-end, including prospecting, negotiations, contracting, launch, performance tracking, and renewals• Create scalable partnership frameworks, playbooks, and processes• Track performance metrics and ROI, continuously refining partnership offerings based on results and insights• Stay ahead of trends within seasonal décor, experiential retail, hospitality, and brand collaborations• Collaborate cross-functionally with marketing, operations, creative, and leadership teams
What you’ll bring:• 15+ years of experience within business development, strategic partnerships, or commercial strategy• Proven success building and scaling enterprise or multi-location partnerships• Strong negotiation skills with experience managing complex stakeholder agreements• Entrepreneurial mindset with the ability to build from the ground up• Excellent communication and presentation skills, with confidence presenting to senior executives• Strategic thinker with the ability to execute and drive deals through to completion
Please apply with your resume for a confidential discussion.
