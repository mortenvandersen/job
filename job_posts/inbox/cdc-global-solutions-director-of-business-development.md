# CDC Global Solutions — Director of Business Development

## Job Description

BD Director/Senior - West Coast - Small Molecule Drug Substance CDMO
CDC are proud to be working with a US based CDMO with recent investments and expansions to their small US team. We are seeking a high-energy, results-driven Business Development Director to serve our clients API development and manufacturing services. This role is primarily responsible for new client acquisition, focusing on identifying, qualifying, and closing new business opportunities to drive aggressive territorial growth. This is a remote, field-based position ideally located in the Western region of the United States reporting directly to the CCO.
Responsibilities
Aggressively prospect for new business opportunities that align with their core technical capabilities in API development and manufacturing.Develop and execute comprehensive sales plans to identify and qualify prospects within the assigned region to achieve new business growth objectives.Actively participate in national tradeshows, exhibits, and professional organizations to generate high-quality leads and increase brand visibility.Organize and lead customer visits to evaluate potential opportunities and present their comprehensive service offerings.Own the end-to-end sales funnel, ensuring a consistent flow of opportunities from initial contact to successful closure.Achieve and exceed annual sales, revenue, and funnel targets to ensure the continued expansion of our market share.Maintain rigorous compliance with reporting processes and CRM tools to provide senior leadership with accurate updates on key initiatives and sales funnel velocity.Collaborate with the Executive Leadership Team (ELT) to construct and execute strategic account plans that maximize growth potential within new and high-value accounts.Lead the negotiation of complex development and supply agreements, working closely with legal and R&D teams to structure winning proposals.Partner with internal technical stakeholders to craft targeted, high-value solutions that directly address the specific API manufacturing needs of the customer.Drive the conversion of proposals into signed business by managing high-level relationships and fostering trust through transparency and partnership.While focusing on acquisition, provide relationship management to ensure new programs move successfully through development stages toward long-term commercialization.
Requirements
Bachelor’s degree in chemistry, Life Sciences, or a related field; an advanced chemistry degree or MBA is highly advantageous.5+ years of proven B2B sales experience, specifically within the contract API or CDMO industry.A demonstrable history of meeting or exceeding new business targets, building sales funnels, and managing complex stakeholder relationships.Exceptional written and verbal communication skills with a mastery of negotiation tactics.
