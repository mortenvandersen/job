# Swooped — Sr. Business Operations Manager

## Job Description

Swooped is a Talent Platform (not a staffing agency). We are not the employer for this role and do not make hiring decisions. Clicking "Apply" takes you to Swooped to review the role and hiring company details.
About the OpportunityThe organization believes that mental health is just as important as physical health. It recognizes that mental health issues can be complex and multifaceted, and is dedicated to treating the whole person, not just the symptoms.
The organization aims to create a world where mental health is no longer stigmatized or marginalized, but rather is embraced as an integral part of one's overall well-being.
The organization believes that by providing quality care that is both evidence-based and compassionate, it can empower individuals to take charge of their mental health and achieve their full potential. The team is passionate about making a positive impact on the lives of those struggling with mental health issues and strives to be a force for positive change in the field of mental healthcare.
This is a remote-first role. The organization currently hires in most U.S. states, with the exception of Hawaii.
About the RoleThe Business Operations team is a group of analytical, operational, and strategic thinkers and executors responsible for helping identify, diagnose, and build solutions for the organization’s highest-priority problems. This team supports the strategic planning process and company operating system, leads cross-functional initiatives and projects, defines and builds company strategy, partners with executives to achieve company OKRs, and ensures leaders constantly have a pulse on the health of the business. The team uses data and qualitative insights to inform the projects to tackle and serves as the primary owner of complex, cross-functional initiatives to drive forward the organization's mission.
The organization is seeking a Sr. Business Operations Manager to join the Business Operations and Strategy Team. This person will primarily be owning strategic and operational projects across the organization. Example historical projects include reducing provider cancellations of initial visits; driving strategy, audits, and fixes to increase referral volume from health plan portals; exploring, evaluating, and recommending new expansion channels and business units to launch (and piloting!); and many others. This person will also likely help run the company operating system and planning cycles in closer partnership with the Head of Business Operations.
This role is pivotal to both making sure the organization runs successfully—ensuring leadership and initiative owners are working on the right objectives with clear alignment and effective forums for decision-making—and delivering on projects aligned to the organization's highest priorities and initiatives.
This individual will bring a mix of skills including strong analytics, strategic thinking, operational excellence, and executive stakeholder and project management to execute the work necessary to take projects from concept to delivery. This individual will operate at all altitudes, from scoping projects and defining the strategy and longer-term vision, to pulling their own data and executing on the smallest details.
