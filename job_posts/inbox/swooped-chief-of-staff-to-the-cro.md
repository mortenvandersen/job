# Swooped — Chief of Staff to the CRO

## Job Description

Swooped is a Talent Platform (not a staffing agency). We are not the employer for this role and do not make hiring decisions. Clicking "Apply" takes you to Swooped to review the role and hiring company details.
About the OpportunityThe company is a global fundraising platform built to make donating to nonprofits fast, seamless, and accessible to all. Every month, its technology powers tens of millions of dollars in donations across the globe. The focus is on innovation that directly impacts results: faster load times, higher conversion rates, global payment support, and accessibility-first design.The platform is trusted by many of the world’s leading nonprofits, including UNICEF, the Alzheimer’s Association, and a wide range of global NGOs. With a 4.9/5 rating across top software review platforms, it is recognized not just for its impact, but for the quality of the product delivered.
About The RoleThe organization is seeking a Chief of Staff to the Chief Revenue Officer to serve as a strategic partner and force multiplier for its global revenue organization. In this high-leverage role, this individual will sit at the intersection of strategy and execution, working alongside the CRO and Executive Leadership Team to orchestrate the go-to-market engine.
As Chief of Staff to the CRO, this individual will define the rhythm of the business, ensuring that the highest-level strategic objectives are translated into measurable outcomes across the organization. This role requires a sophisticated operator capable of navigating complex organizational dynamics and acting as a trusted advisor to senior leadership during a period of rapid institutional scaling. The individual will be responsible for converting vision into velocity, ensuring that every part of the revenue engine is aligned, informed, and executing at peak performance.
Key ResponsibilitiesAct as a strategic partner to the CRO, driving high-priority operational initiatives across Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, RevOps, and Partnerships.Convert high-level vision into actionable, measurable execution plans, ensuring clear accountability and resource alignment across the GTM organization.Drive the completion of mission-critical special projects and action items, acting as a proxy for the CRO to maintain organizational velocity.Design and manage the core GTM operating model, including pipeline reviews, forecasting cycles, QBRs, and leadership offsites.Proactively identify systemic friction points and architect scalable solutions to improve organizational efficiency.Collaborate with Revenue Operations to synthesize complex performance metrics into actionable insights for the executive team.Serve as the primary bridge between the GTM organization and Product, Finance, and People teams to ensure seamless cross-functional execution.Connect the dots across disparate workstreams to identify risks, dependencies, and opportunities that impact the broader revenue strategy.Lead the preparation of high-stakes materials for Board of Directors meetings, Executive Leadership sessions, and investor relations.Structure executive discussions to facilitate rapid, data-informed decision-making and ensure alignment on complex strategic trade-offs.Orchestrate the internal communication strategy for the CRO, ensuring clarity of mission and transparency of progress across the global GTM organization.Own end-to-end execution of critical GTM workstreams, including territory optimization, segmentation, and new market entries.Pilot and institutionalize new revenue motions, moving them from concept to scalable process.Act as a strike team leader to diagnose and resolve systemic bottlenecks across the revenue engine.
