# RemoteHunter — Senior Manager, Transformation

## Job Description

1. About Our Client:The organization operates in the pest control and related services industry, serving consumer and business customers across multiple sectors in more than 90 countries. It addresses challenges related to pest management while maintaining a focus on protecting people, enhancing lives, and preserving the environment. The company supports many leading global brands and emphasizes a legacy of consistent operational excellence.
2. About the Opportunity:The Senior Manager, Transformation leads large-scale change initiatives, partnering strategically with business leaders to drive projects from concept through implementation. This role ensures alignment of project goals with the organization’s near-term objectives and long-term strategy, coordinating across North American functional leaders. The position is central to achieving successful program outcomes through comprehensive program management, including planning, stakeholder coordination, governance, and change management.
3. Responsibilities:• Develop long-term transformation roadmaps aligned with 3 to 5-year strategic goals• Manage the full lifecycle of complex programs from diagnosis to implementation and handoff• Define program scope, success criteria, KPIs, deliverables, resources, and alignment with strategic goals• Coordinate with stakeholders and subject matter experts to ensure alignment, manage dependencies, and escalate decisions• Identify and manage risks effectively• Enforce governance and reporting standards to maintain accountability• Incorporate field insights through ride-alongs, interviews, and advisory boards in solution design• Collaborate with enterprise change management for effective communication and implementation• Measure program success against original business cases and demonstrate ROI• Develop strategies that account for project interdependencies within programs and ongoing operations• Identify resource needs and secure necessary support, including business case development• Maintain and improve program and project management tools, templates, and processes• Ensure project managers maintain documentation and report progress• Lead and participate in meetings to drive program success and report status• Deliver clear, accurate communications tailored to the audience
4. Requirements:
Education• Bachelor’s degree in Business or related field, or equivalent professional experience• Master’s degree preferred
Experience• 8 to 12+ years in management consulting, corporate strategy, or program management• 5+ years of management experience preferred
Skills & Competencies• Strong commercial acumen with a customer service focus• Analytical and problem-solving skills with an understanding of key metrics• Proven people management skills leading large, dispersed, diverse teams across multiple locations• Strong organizational and leadership abilities, effective both strategically and operationally• Excellent communication and influencing skills at all organizational levels with varied stakeholders• High drive, work ethic, personal accountability, and ability to perform under pressure with sound judgment• Integrity and ability to build trusting relationships with key stakeholders
5. Pay Range and Compensation Package:• Base pay range: $125,300 - $162,800 per year• Pay varies based on location, skills, education, and experience• Total earnings may include overtime, incentives, commissions, performance bonuses, and other factors
6. Benefits & Perks:• Benefits begin on day one for full-time employees with no waiting period
