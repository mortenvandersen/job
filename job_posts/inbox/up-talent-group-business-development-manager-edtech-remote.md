# UP Talent Group — Business Development Manager - EdTech (Remote)

## Job Description

UP Talent Group is partnering with an early-stage company dedicated to transforming K-8 education. The company is a national leader in the technology-enabled STEAM education space.
The company is growing and has several openings for dynamic Business Development Managers to help expand outreach and drive new business initiatives. They have an exceptional leadership team and are on a mission to design a STEAM curriculum that transforms learners into innovators through STEAM Project-based learning.
If you are a dynamic Business Development Manager with experience working with K-8 on the West Coast US and are passionate about impacting the education field, this could be an exciting opportunity.

As a Business Development Manager, You Will:
Identify and pursue new business opportunities in the K-8 STEM/STEAM education market, including schools, districts, and educational organizations.Develop and execute a comprehensive business development strategy to achieve annual sales targets.Collaborate with marketing and product teams to align strategies and create compelling value propositions for potential clients.Cold outreach, build and maintain relationships with key decision-makers in the education sector, including school administrators, educators, and policymakers.Conduct market research to identify trends, competitor activities, and potential challenges in primary and lower-secondary education.Prepare and deliver engaging presentations and demonstrations of our products and solutions to prospective clients.Negotiate contracts and agreements with new partners and clients, ensuring favorable terms for the company.Track and analyze business development metrics and report on progress to the leadership team.

What They Value in You:
Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Education, Marketing, or a related field.5+ years of experience in business development and sales within the EdTech or K-8 education sector.Proven track record of meeting or exceeding sales targets (quotes of $500,000 and higher) and developing successful business strategies.Strong understanding of the K-8 education landscape, including trends, challenges, and opportunities.Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to engage and influence diverse stakeholders.Self-motivated, results-oriented, and able to work independently as well as collaboratively within a team.Proficient in CRM software (HubSpot) and Microsoft Office Suite.Specifically focuses on the Western regions of the US.Willing and flexible to travel on an as-needed basis.

What They Appreciate:
STEM/STEAM sales experience.Experience working for early-stage or start-ups.Experience with data analysis tools.Mission-driven, dedicated, and would like to be in a fast-paced and driven environment.

What They Offer:
Competitive Base Salary and attractive performance-based incentives/commission.A vibrant, collaborative, and inclusive work environment dedicated to making a positive impact on education.
