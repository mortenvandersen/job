# Nibbi Brothers General Contractors — Marketing Director

## Job Description

Nibbi SummaryNibbi Brothers has been a member of the Bay Area’s construction community since 1950. The Company is known for integrity, innovation, quality and contributions to the communities in which it works. Nibbi’s safety program often exceeds federal and state requirements, prioritizing people's well-being. Market sector experience includes multi-family residential, aviation, infrastructure, pre-fab/modular, commercial, seismic retrofits, historic restoration, waterfront structures, and non-profit community facilities. Nibbi self-performs production framing and structural concrete. Our projects range in size from $5 million to over $200 million.Nibbi provides its people with comprehensive benefits and opportunities for career development. We encourage continuing education at all levels. We are proud of our friendly, family culture, based on respect, collaboration and a strong work/life balance. 
Position Summary and Essential FunctionsPartner with the VP of Business Development and Marketing to develop and be responsible for executing marketing strategies to help achieve Nibbi’s business goals.Be a strategic partner to other Nibbi divisions and departments, such as Prefab, HR, Safety, and Preconstruction and Operations, for converting opportunities into wins.Serve as a pursuit strategist by leading the RFQ/RFP process and preparing for client interviews.Ensure the quality of work product is consistent with Nibbi’s standards of excellence; is responsive to the clients’ needs and reflects the company’s brand and values.Balance strategic leadership with hands-on involvement and a roll up your sleeves mentality.Ensure the marketing team operates with cohesion, clear direction, purpose and a vision for the future while maintaining a strong team connection and culture.
Responsibilities General Execute the existing marketing plan and develop all future plans and growth needs in alignment with Nibbi’s business goals.Oversee branding standards, messaging, and the integration of Nibbi’s values in all client-facing materials.Oversees strategy and content calendar for email and social media marketing.Ensure consistency and quality across proposals, presentations, qualifications, and corporate marketing collateral.Oversees all PR planning and outreach.Work with VP of Business Development and Marketing to develop and maintain the Marketing Plan.Monitor market trends, client expectations, and competitive intelligence to inform marketing and pursuit strategies.Maintain accurate data for pursuit pipelines and marketing performance indicators.Seek feedback and integrate lessons learned into future pursuits and marketing efforts.Lead pursuit debriefs, client feedback sessions, and post-award analyses to drive continuous improvement.Participate in industry events and professional organizations to engage with thought leaders and maintain an awareness of trends and market conditions.

Proposal ManagementLead kick-off sessions and develop proposal win strategies, including identifying key differentiators and Nibbi value adds, alongside the proposal staff team members.Prepare executives and project teams for client interviews, presentations, and shortlist meetings.Responsible for end-to-end proposal production.Champion innovation in proposal strategy and storytelling.Develop an effective proposal process, templates, tools, and best practices. Manage excellence in the quality of content and production.Ensure content aligns with the pursuit strategy and presents a clear, persuasive narrative. Ensure proposals are complete and comply with instructions in the RFP/RFQ. 
Team Leadership & DevelopmentEstablish clear roles, workflows, and performance expectations. Cultivate a high-performing, engaged team by recognizing individual strengths, advocating for growth opportunities, and providing mentorship.
QualificationsEducation & ExperienceBachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communication, Construction Management, or related field required; Master’s preferred.10+ years of progressive marketing and proposal leadership experience in construction, engineering, or professional services.Experience with private and public sector clients.Experience with growth and regional expansion/division planning.Understanding of construction terminology and delivery methods, including Progressive Design-Build, Design-Build, CM/GC, Best-Value, Hard-Bid/Lump Sum and other alternative methods.
Skills & CompetenciesStrong business acumen and understanding of construction operations.Proficient at marketing strategy, branding, and business development integration.Exceptional writing, editing, and storytelling skills in the AEC environment, ideally for a GC.Demonstrated success leading complex pursuits and improving win rates.Strong planning and project management skills.Navigates change with agility.Proficiency with Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suite, CRM systems (e.g., Unanet preferred), and proposal automation tools (e.g., Joist.ai).
