# XDemics Corporation — Director Sales and Commercial Development

## Job Description

POSITION OVERVIEWXDemics Corporation, a recently emerged Caltech spinout, has developed a high-density, natural cell growth technology that mimics how cells grow in the human body.  By overcoming longstanding oxygen delivery and cell stress limitations, the platform supports healthier and more efficient production of complex therapies including CAR-T, stem cells, and gene therapies, as well as several adjacencies, including cells for biomedical R&D, HTS, biologics, and 3D culture. We are building a high-performance team to scale sales & sales operations to support rapid commercial growth.
We are seeking a highly organized, proactive Director of Sales and Commercial Development, with significant sales experience in the cell and gene therapy bioprocessing space, who thrives in a fast-paced, startup environment. The ideal candidate takes ownership, anticipates needs, and solves problems before they escalate. You bring strong attention to detail, clear communication and collaboration skills, and the ability to manage multiple priorities at once. You value professionalism, discretion, and accountability, and you take pride in keeping people, processes, and information running smoothly.
