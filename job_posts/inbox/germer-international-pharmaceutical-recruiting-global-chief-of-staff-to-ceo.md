# Germer International - Pharmaceutical Recruiting — Global Chief of Staff  to CEO

## Job Description

**This person will need to live in either Florida or Dallas, TX

Our client is a global, pharmaceutical leader, with a diverse portfolio of approved products across various therapeutic areas such as hematology, oncology, and antibiotics. As they expand their US presence, they are looking for a Chief of Staff we has previously supported a CEO in the pharmaceutical industry. The Chief of Staff will not only be responsible for supporting the CEO on all global administrative functions, but will also be responsible for managing 3 Executive Assistants across the globe.
