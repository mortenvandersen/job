# American Express — Product Director - Agentic Commerce, Strategy and Insights

## Job Description

Job Description

The GMNS Product team is at the heart of American Express and is a core function within the Global Merchant and Network Services (GMNS) business unit. Each year, we power billions of transactions through our global payments network, serving our Issuing & Acquiring partners in more than 170 markets worldwide. We grow revenue by driving strategic, multi-year initiatives to deliver differentiated value to our merchants, partners, and customers.

GMNS Product is responsible for the Network, the maintenance of existing functionality, the development of new products and services, and operations. Our solutions deliver value to American Express’ Card Members, acquirers and partners across the globe, whether proprietary or third-party by facilitating secure and seamless transactions between American Express’ issuers, acquirers and merchants. Our goal is to enable Card Members to pay however and wherever they choose. We deliver on these by setting the vision and strategy for the Network, anchoring our priorities into those of the enterprise, and developing and managing an associated set of products. We keep the customer perspective top-of-mind, factor in competition and regulatory trends, and apply an end-to-end product management approach (i.e., including strategy, roadmaps, scaling plans, business cases, success metrics, KPIs). We lead the end-to-end process, manage the end-to-end partner lifecycle and are also responsible for industry intelligence and engagement through various governing bodies.

The Network Agentic Solutions team is responsible for defining and enabling the future of Agentic Commerce across the Amex Global Network. As AI-driven agents, digital wallets, and automated commerce experiences evolve, we are shaping how the Network supports secure, scalable, and commercially compelling agent-based transactions. Our focus spans tokenization, digital identity, and emerging network capabilities required to power next-generation commerce.

The Director, Strategy & Insights, will play a critical role in shaping the commercial and strategic direction of Network Agentic Solutions. Reporting to the Vice President, Network Agentic Solutions, this leader will drive commercial strategy, competitive intelligence, and performance insights to inform multi-year planning, partner prioritization, and enterprise alignment. This role will lead a team and operate through strong cross-functional collaboration across Product, Risk, Finance, Enterprise Strategy, and regional teams.

Responsibilities

How will you make an impact in this role?

 Support development of the multi-year strategy for Network Agentic Solutions, translating industry dynamics and commercial insights into actionable strategic priorities.  Lead commercial strategy development, including pricing frameworks, partner prioritization, and business case assessments.  Drive global competitive and industry intelligence across networks, wallets, AI-driven commerce models, emerging standards bodies (e.g., EMVCo), and regulatory developments.  Synthesize external trends and internal performance data into clear implications and executive-ready materials.  Own consolidated KPI tracking and performance insights for Network Agentic Solutions, identifying risks and growth opportunities.  Develop executive-level presentations, strategic narratives, and enterprise communications to ensure alignment across senior stakeholders.  Partner closely with Enterprise Strategy, Risk, Finance, Product, and regional teams to inform prioritization and partnership evaluations.  Lead and develop a high-performing team, fostering structured thinking, commercial rigor, and external insights. 

Qualifications

Required Qualifications

 6+ years of experience in payments, financial services, or strategy roles, with strong understanding of global network economics and emerging digital commerce models.  Demonstrated experience in commercial strategy, pricing, and partner prioritization.  Strong industry and competitive intelligence capabilities, with the ability to translate market developments into enterprise implications.  Solid financial acumen, including business case development and scenario modeling.  Experience influencing senior stakeholders within a global, matrixed organization.  Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to distill complexity into clear, compelling narratives.  Proven ability to lead and develop small, high-performing teams.  This role requires a commercially minded, externally curious leader who thrives in ambiguity and brings structured thinking to emerging opportunities. The ideal candidate combines strategic insight with practical execution, enabling American Express to lead in the evolution of Agentic Commerce.
