# Ladders — SVP, Outside the Base Sales

## Job Description

For our client, we are seeking a SVP Outside The Base Sales to join the team of a leader in the Healthcare space. This role will support revenue growth through customer focus, relationship development, and disciplined commercial execution. You will collaborate with sales, customer, product, and operational partners to identify opportunities, solve problems, and improve outcomes. The position offers the opportunity to strengthen market execution, customer relationships, and measurable business results within a healthcare environment.

Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

Compensation: $180,000 – $250,000 annually

Responsibilities

 Lead the strategy and execution of outside-the-base sales to achieve revenue goals Establish sales quotas and territory strategies aligned with business objectives Develop and manage a high-performing sales team responsible for acquiring new customers Create accurate sales forecasts and performance metrics for strategic planning Drive disciplined sales processes and the adoption of relevant technologies Assess market conditions and customer needs to inform sales strategy Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop go-to-market strategies

Qualifications

 Bachelor’s degree in Business, Healthcare Administration, Marketing, or a related field Master’s degree or MBA preferred 12+ years of progressive sales experience, particularly in healthcare sales 5+ years of recent leadership experience in a sales-driven environment Experience leveraging sales technology and analytics for improved outcomes

Benefits

 Opportunities for executive-level relationship building with clients Exposure to advanced sales methodologies utilizing AI and data analytics Potential for considerable influence over company-wide strategic initiatives Involvement in expanding market reach within the healthcare sector

Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
