# Visa — Director, Global Markets Operations – Chief of Staff

## Job Description

About Us

Visa is a world leader in payments technology, facilitating transactions between consumers, merchants, financial institutions and government entities across more than 200 countries and territories, dedicated to uplifting everyone, everywhere by being the best way to pay and be paid.

At Visa, you'll have the opportunity to create impact at scale — tackling meaningful challenges, growing your skills and seeing your contributions impact lives around the world.

Join Visa and do work that matters – to you, to your community, and to the world. Progress starts with you.

Job Description

The Director, Global Markets Operations – Chief of Staff is a key member of the Global Markets Operations team. They will focus on both strategic and execution-oriented topics and serve as Chief of Staff to Visa’s Group President.

Strategic topics may include: Global coordination on key risks facing multiple regions (e.g., A2A, RTP, Wallets, Regulatory activity, Localization), consideration of new business and operating models, identification of key trends impacting our business and thought leadership re: these local payment experiences, Sales Transformation initiatives, optimizing and streamlining corporate processes, representing the President and/or voice of the Regions across Visa-wide initiatives, etc.   

Execution-oriented topics may include: BoD presentations, EC reporting on key business updates across the Global Markets team, OKR tracking, crafting presentations for the President on key business topics and global payments trends for both external and internal audiences, Quarterly Business Reviews facilitation, Budget facilitation, crafting agendas and content for the President’s market visits around the world, planning for key events like Visa’s Annual Market Kick-Off, etc. 

Across all topics, the role will require highly effective collaboration and stakeholder engagement across a wide array of functions – incl. collaborating with senior leaders (key stakeholders include but are not limited to: Regional Presidents, the Global Markets Leadership Team, Regional and Functional BPOs, Group Country Managers and Country Managers, Regional Leadership teams, Global Sales and Commercial Operations, the Office of the CEO, Finance, Legal, Corporate Strategy, Global and Regional Product teams, and more).

Responsibilities Include

Serving as ‘Chief of Staff’ to Group President, including owning and prioritizing the running list or backlog of topics relevant for the President and the Global Markets team Supporting the management of the Group President and the Global Markets Leadership Team’s operating models (market visits, global corporate calendar, process optimization etc.) Executing against the top priorities for the President and the Global Markets team Preparation of materials for key external and internal forums – including slide deck creation and scripting for the Group President and contributing to the knowledge of key market themes/trends Engaging with and establishing relationships with stakeholders across most teams at Visa across a range of priority topics, including close partnership with Regional BPO teams, Global Markets Leadership Team BPO teams, and CEO Office Assisting in the development of perspectives on a range of priority strategic initiatives as indicated by Group President Crafting the agenda and working with stakeholders on content for the President’s key internal forums: The Global Markets Leadership team (meetings, Onsites), the Market Leadership Council, etc. Collecting data on financial and other business performance and synthesizing outcomes Preparing Group President for client and government meetings/conferences (e.g., Davos) Preparation of materials for BoD meetings on key business updates Serve as people leader – this role has direct report(s) Peer mentorship across the Global Markets Operations team, helping build out the team over time, continuing to refine the remit of the function, and acting as a key leader within the Ops team and as a representative of the President’s Office 

This is a hybrid position. Expectation of days in office will be confirmed by your Hiring Manager.
