# Confidential — Director of Business Development

## Job Description

About UsWe are a newly formed specialty finance firm focused on a singular, time-sensitive opportunity: advancing funds to U.S. importers against their court-ordered IEEPA tariff refunds. Following the Supreme Court’s February 2026 ruling striking down IEEPA tariffs and the Court of International Trade’s subsequent refund orders, over 300,000 importers are owed a share of approximately $166 billion in refunds — but face an uncertain and protracted timeline to actually receive their money. We provide importers with immediate capital against their confirmed refund claims, handling the administrative recovery process end-to-end.
We are backed by experienced leadership in finance, law, and operations, and we are building the team that will define this category.
The Opportunity﻿This is a first-mover opportunity in a market that didn’t exist six months ago. There is no established competitor offering advance funding against IEEPA tariff refunds. The addressable market is enormous — hundreds of thousands of importers, tens of billions of dollars in confirmed refunds, and a government processing system that will take months (or longer) to clear the backlog. The Director of Business Development will be the commercial engine of this business.
