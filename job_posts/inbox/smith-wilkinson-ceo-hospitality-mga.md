# Smith & Wilkinson — CEO, Hospitality MGA

## Job Description

Our specialty MGA client is seeking a dynamic Chief Executive Officer to lead their growing platform into its next phase of development. This hands-on, general manager–style role is ideal for a commercial operator ready to scale and modernize a business with strong underwriting talent, established specialty programs, and a meaningful market presence.
The CEO will drive growth by expanding distribution, deepening broker relationships, developing new products, and elevating brand recognition, while continuing to support core industries such as aviation, cannabis, hospitality, student housing, and workers’ compensation (a current $100mill+ GWP portfolio).
With a seasoned existing team providing operational continuity and the backing of committed ownership, the incoming leader will shape strategy, implement AI-enabled underwriting tools, standardize technology-driven workflows, and pursue strategic M&A opportunities.You must have at least 15 years of P&L management of various departments (underwriting, operations, claims, finance, technology), a proven track record expanding distribution across retailers and wholesalers, product expertise in hospitality, cannabis, or aviation, and direct experience at an MGA or within a programs division to be considered.
You must have a minimum of 15 years of commercial property & casualty insurance experience to be considered. No one from outside industries will be considered, do not apply if you do not have true insurance experience. 
Position is fully remote with frequent visits to their HQ in the Midwest. This is a great opportunity for a P&L leader looking to step up into their first CEO role.
Contact Harrison Hines or Stacy St. Onge to learn more.
