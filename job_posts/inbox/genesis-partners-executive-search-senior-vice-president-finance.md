# Genesis Partners - Executive Search — Senior Vice President Finance

## Job Description

Senior Vice President, FinanceFully Remote | West Coast PreferredConfidential Search
Genesis Partners - Executive Search is conducting a confidential search for a Senior Vice President of Finance on behalf of a private equity backed, rapidly scaling wellness CPG company. The business has established strong brand momentum, a highly engaged customer base, and is entering a critical phase of operational and financial expansion.
This is a senior leadership role designed for a finance executive who wants genuine influence on company trajectory. The mandate is not simply reporting accuracy or accounting oversight. The SVP Finance will serve as a core strategic partner to the executive team and investors, shaping decisions that directly impact growth, capital efficiency, and long term enterprise value.
Role Overview
The SVP Finance will own the finance function at the highest level, with responsibility spanning financial planning and analysis, liquidity management, performance modeling, and executive level decision support. This individual will operate at the intersection of strategy and execution, translating financial insights into clear operating priorities.
The environment requires a builder mentality. The company is scaling quickly, and finance plays a central role in navigating growth, complexity, and investment decisions. This is a role for someone comfortable with both altitude and detail.
Key Responsibilities
Lead company wide financial planning, forecasting, and long range modelingDrive performance analysis across revenue, margins, customer economics, and channel dynamicsOwn cash management, liquidity planning, and capital allocation disciplinePartner closely with the CEO and executive team on strategic initiatives and operating decisionsDevelop board and investor materials, including financial narratives and KPI frameworksEstablish scalable financial infrastructure, processes, and controls aligned with company growthEvaluate growth investments, commercial initiatives, and operational tradeoffsSupport fundraising, lender relationships, and investor interactions as neededBuild, mentor, and scale the finance organization over time
Candidate Profile
This role is intentionally structured for a high caliber finance leader with demonstrated experience operating in dynamic, growth oriented environments.
Strong candidates will typically possess:
Experience within private equity, growth equity, or high growth operating companiesDeep expertise in FP&A, financial modeling, and business performance analyticsClear understanding of cash flow dynamics and capital efficiency in scaling businessesAbility to operate as a strategic partner rather than a functional specialistConfidence interacting with executive leadership and investorsHigh intellectual horsepower paired with pragmatic business judgmentComfort navigating ambiguity, rapid change, and evolving priorities
Industry background within wellness, consumer products, subscription models, or broader CPG environments is highly relevant but not strictly required. The defining factor is operating capability and financial acumen.
Why This Role Is Distinct
This is not a maintenance oriented finance position. The SVP Finance will play a central role in guiding company evolution, influencing growth strategy, and shaping major business decisions. The leadership team views finance as a value creation function, not a reporting function.
The opportunity offers meaningful exposure to executive decision making, investor dynamics, and enterprise building.
Structure & Compensation
Fully remote role, preference for West Coast due to executive team footprintCash compensation up to $290K + meaningful equity participation
If this aligns with your background, or you know a finance leader who would find this compelling, inquiries can be made confidentially.
