# Mixpanel — Senior Revenue Strategy & Operations Manager

## Job Description

About Mixpanel: Mixpanel is an event analytics platform for builders who need answers from their data at their fingertips — no SQL required. Over 9,000 paid customers, including Netflix, Pinterest, Sweetgreen, and Samsara, use Mixpanel to understand their customers and measure progress. Funded by Andreessen-Horowitz, Sequoia, YC, and Bain Capital ($277M+ raised).

About the Role: As Senior Revenue Strategy & Operations Manager, you'll support on defining the strategy behind customer retention and account growth. You'll partner closely with CS and Sales leadership to improve Gross and Net Revenue Retention, enable scalable expansion motions, and proactively identify areas to enhance the customer experience and lifetime value.

Responsibilities:
- Drive strategic initiatives that shape how we engage, retain, and grow our customer base
- Conduct advanced market, customer, and product analyses to uncover whitespace opportunities and guide resource allocation
- Own and evolve the reporting framework for sales and post-sales KPIs
- Act as a strategic thought partner to GTM leadership — bringing analytical rigor to inform key decisions
- Provide high-impact operational support to the Sales organization
- Run and evolve key operating cadences, including business performance deep dives and executive-level business reviews

We're Looking for Someone Who Has:
- 3+ years of experience at a top tier Management or Strategy Consulting firm
- 2+ years of operating experience in Corporate Strategy, Business Operations, or Sales Strategy in a high-growth B2B SaaS organization
- Experience defining and operationalizing GTM Strategy with proven experience driving impact on retention and expansion metrics (GRR, NRR, etc.)
- Strong project management experience with demonstrated ability to manage time, prioritize tasks and work within deadlines
- Track-record of collaborating with cross-functional partners to execute projects

Bonus Points For:
- Working knowledge of SFDC including Reporting and Record Management
- Proficiency in modeling and analyzing complex and large data sets
- Demonstrable passion for the data analytics industry

Compensation: $189,000 - $231,000 TTCC (Total Target Cash Compensation — includes base + variable)
