## Company Preferences

### Stage & Size
- **Best fit**: Growth-stage startups, 100+ employees, with demonstrated product-market fit and strong growth trajectory
- **Also open to**: VC-backed companies, PE-backed firms, large enterprises (experience at LEGO means large corp environments are comfortable)
- **At larger companies**: Interested in owning a specific product line or business unit representing a new market or growth bet — not just a seat in a mature org

### Culture
- Collaborative, friendly, and ambitious
- Mission-driven with a vision beyond near-term profit — but commercially focused and grounded in real targets
- Not purely financially-oriented (not a fit for pure holding companies or cost-optimization mandates)

### Industry

**Tier 1 — Will prioritize even for less-than-perfect role fit:**
- Education Technology (EdTech)
  - Higher Ed, K–12, CTE / vocational training, corporate learning & development, healthcare / nursing education

**Tier 2 — Strong interest:**
- AI and technology broadly
- Consumer entertainment
- Companies in non-EdTech industries that have a meaningful education product or unit
- Environmental / sustainability tech _(no direct experience, but a strong personal draw)_

**Tier 3 — Open if role fit is strong:**
- Most other industries

**Excluded:**
- Oil & gas, tobacco, gambling — and other industries with clear ethical conflicts
